# What are the different types of pull-ups and pulldowns?

What are the different types of pulldown/pullup variations? Which is the best for muscle growth?

when performing different types of pullups/pulldowns there are different joint actions occurring. this consists of shoulder adduction, shoulder extension and some elbow flexion. so how can you determine what exercises to pick?

adduction extension flexion

when doing a wider grip pull in the frontal plane you will be training shoulder adduction.

when doing a neutral grip pull where your arms are more tucked you will be training shoulder extension.

when doing a close grip pull with a tucked/underhand grip you will be training shoulder extension with a lot of elbow flexion alongside it.

so now that you know all the exercises how can you determine which you should do? i would recommend having both a shoulder adduction challenge and shoulder extension challenge in your session. i would also avoid using exercises that load elbow flexion a lot as you should be already training your biceps/elbow flexors directly.
