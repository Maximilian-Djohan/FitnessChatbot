# What is the best front delt exercise?

What is the BEST front delt exercise

to train the front delt we want to train it through either of its functions: shoulder flexion or shoulder abduction where it has best relative leverage in comparison to other muscles that share those functions. we can do this below 20 degrees of shoulder flexion. what is a good exercise for this?

we do this with a cuffed front raise below 20 degrees. with it being hardest around ~10 degrees of shoulder flexion.

alternatively if you don't want to do a front raise you can just shoulder press above 100 degrees to train shoulder abduction. (better if it's ascending)
