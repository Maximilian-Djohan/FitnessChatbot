# does caffeine help with muscle growth?

Does Caffeine improve muscle growth? How much Caffeine should you consume?

Caffeine is known as something that helps you stay awake/energized and is commonly found in preworkout and energy drinks. but does it actually have an impact on your training and muscle growth?

Caffeine boosts your performance by increasing your maximum tolerable perceived effort. this makes it so you will be able to tolerate more effort before you reach your maximum perception of effort. this has the purpose of increasing motor unit recruitment and in turn muscle growth.

The maximum tolerable perception of effort is the limiting factor for a strength training set, because reaching this point prevents further increases in effort.

If taking some form of caffeine it should be done around 60 minutes prior to training for it to peak in the bloodstream. and for the amount you should take it should be around 13 milligrams per pound of body weight. i also wouldnt recommend taking it in the afternoon as it can negatively impact sleep.

For most healthy adults, the Tolerable Upper Intake Level (UL) of caffeine is 400 milligrams per day.
