# the back extension is not a back exercise?

The back extension is NOT a back exercise its actually a leg exercise

many people do a back extension thinking it will work their lower back but this isnt the case. a back extension is actually training hip extension which will train your legs. your lower back will only be training through stabilization in this as you arent actively training spinal extension.

so when youre doing hip extension you will actually be training your hamstrings, glutes and adductor magnus as they extend the hip. another common example of this would be in something like an SLDL where you are also training hip extension
