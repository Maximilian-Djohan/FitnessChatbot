# Serratus Anterior Training Explained

Serratus Anterior
Training Explained

The serratus anterior is the gill like muscle on sides of your body. to train them you want to perform their main functions. they perform two functions being scapular protraction and upwards rotation of the scapula. how do we train this?

PROTRACTION UPWARD ROTATION

to train protraction you can have your arms fully extended in a pressing setup then push your shoulders forward to protract the scapula.

to train upwards rotation you can have your arms fully extended in a shoulder press setup and push your shoulders upwards.
