# the decline press is overhated

Is the decline press is over hated and is actually good for chest growth?

many people say that a decline press is useless and all you need is an incline and flat movement for your chest. this is right and wrong. most people will only need an upper pec exercise like an incline press and mid/lower pec exercise like a pec dec. but there actually is a use for the decline press.

first you need to see how you are performing your decline press. your arm path should be different then your flat press. instead of horizontal adduction bringing your arm directly infront of your body you should be vertically adducting your arms bringing them to the side of your body similar to a lat pulldown. but why is this?

towards the end range of adduction your chest will actually have better leverage over your lats. so through training vertical adduction of the chest you could train different fibers of your sternal/costal pec that arent hit in your horizontal adduction exercise.

so should you add a decline movement in your training? for most people no but if you wanted to train another region of your chest after your other movements this can be something extra to add.
