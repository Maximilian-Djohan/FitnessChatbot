# what does it mean to be type one fiber dominant?

What does it mean to be type 1 fiber dominant? How can you tell if you're type 1 fiber dominant?

People in recent times say they are type 1 fiber dominant, meaning their muscles are made up of a higher proportion of type 1 fibers which have less growth potential. So if you have a higher proportion of type 1 fibers your growth will be slower and your maximum potential will be lower. But how can you actually know if you are type 1 fiber dominant?

Leg extension power and muscle fiber composition

The only way to test it is through a muscle biopsy. They will take out a sample of your muscle tissue and determine the proportion of type 1 to type 2 muscle fibers.
