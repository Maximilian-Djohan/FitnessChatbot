# do rest days increase muscle growth?

Do rest days actually increase muscle growth? What are the purpose of rest days?

people take rest days periodically in the week usually after hitting every muscle group a certain amount eg ULR or ULULR, etc. to let their body recover. but do rest days actually help with muscle growth?

every time you train you will have muscle damage that will cause CNS fatigue even if you are training different muscles. this CNS fatigue will continue to impair next sessions unless you take a rest day. depending on your volume/frequency it might not make an impact immediately but it eventually will be enough to be a noticeable detriment. so what does this mean?

Central nervous system (CNS) fatigue is likely greater during strength training when lifting light loads than when using more moderate loads. Recovery is slower when training with light loads compared to heavy loads?

SUMMARY

this CNS fatigue will impair MUR and in turn lead to less muscle growth. so at the very least you should take 1-2 rest days throughout the week. and yes no one is going to force you to rest but that doesnt mean its any better to not take rest days.
