# is it possible for you to hit 200lbs in your lifting journey

is it possible for YOU to hit 200lbs throughout your lifting lifetime? If so how long would it take?

many people like to set an arbitrary weight goal for them to hit because of the physiques they see online. a common end goal people have is around this 180-200lb range. but is it actually possible for you to hit this and how long would it take?

well first it isnt the best to have a weight goal as many people chasing a weight end up just getting fat. you put on muscle GRAMS at a time which will end up being around ~1lb a month as a beginner and as you get advanced it can be half or even a quarter of this amount. ignoring that what would the timeframe of getting to that 180-200lb look like?

well keeping the rate of building muscle in mind and using FFMI (fat free body mass index) we can make estimates. i made a chart using the estimates that in your first two years of training you will put ~1lb a month. towards the middle of your training ~.5lbs per month. then as you get advanced ~.3lbs per month. given a rough estimate it will take a 5'10 150 lbs ~18ffmi (beginner) around ~6.5 to get to 200lbs reaching your natty limit of a ~24ffmi
