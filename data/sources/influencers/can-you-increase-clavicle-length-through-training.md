# can you increase clavicle length through training?

Can you increase the length of your Clavicles through training?

its often seen as aesthetic to have wider clavicles as its supposed to give a more appealing frame. with broad shoulders in comparison to your waist. some people say that you can increase your clavicle length through training with something like a Reeves deadlift but is this true?

you cant actually train your clavicles to grow but if you want to create that illusion of having a wider frame training your side delts and lats will give you the illusion of having a better frame.
