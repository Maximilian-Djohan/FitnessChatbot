# Is there a difference between the seated and lying hamstring curl?

is there a difference between a seated vs lying hamstring curl? is one better for muscle growth?

both of these exercises will train all four of your hamstring muscles entirely but a seated leg curl is more shortened focused (towards lockout) while a seated hamstring is lengthened focus. so is one better than another?

the seated curl trains more of the bicep femoris longhead, semimembranosus, and the semitendinosus as they will be influenced by the bent hip position when seated while the lying curl trains more of the biceps femoris short head as it isn't influenced by a bent hip. so what exercise should you choose?

while they are both GREAT options the seated curl will be slightly better. but you shouldn't worry if you only have access to one of them as they are still both good options to train the hamstring.

alongside your hamstring curl which trains the hamstring more distally (closer to the knee) you should also include a hip extension exercise like an SLDL/Hyperextension to train your hamstring more proximally (closer to the hip)
