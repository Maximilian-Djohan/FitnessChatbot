# how does foot placement affect your squat pattern?

How does foot placement alter your squat pattern? Is there a "best" foot placement for muscle growth?

some people say that your foot placement can determine what youre working in your leg press. but is somewhat true. no matter what you will be training hip extension and knee extension training the muscles that perform those functions. but how does foot placement affect this?

taking a low foot stance will allow for more knee flexion compared to hip flexion which will result in more of a quad bias (choose width that is comfortable and dont go too low)

taking a higher foot stance will allow for more hip extension compared to knee extension which will result with more of an adductors/glute bias

so which foot position should you pick? well no matter what you will work all muscles that are involved through knee extension and hip extension but you can pick a stance which will help you bias the muscles you want to prioritize
