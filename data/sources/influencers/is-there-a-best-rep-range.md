there has been many different rep ranges over the years being used as the best for muscle growth from low to high reps. but is there actually a best range for muscle growth?

The best rep range for hypertrophy (muscle growth) is generally considered to be 6-12 reps per set, according to some fitness publications.

the only thing that matters for muscle growth is that you have a high degree of effort and mechanical tension. this will happen as long as you train to a close proximity of failure/to failure. no matter the rep range the last 5 will be the most stimulating regardless if you do 5 or 30 reps.
