# How should you fix a muscle imbalance?

How should you fix a muscle imbalance?

You should use unilateral movements and train to failure/close to failure on your weakside first then go straight into your strong side. if the imbalance is severe match the reps you got on your weakside to your strongside.

weakside: 7 reps 0 rir strongside: 7 reps 4 rir

Some people might have the question about what they should do if one side is bigger but isnt stronger then the other side. This is actually not a muscle imbalance but instead just a visual difference based on your genetics. your muscles arent symmetrical and its fine to have this.

variation in the size, shape, or appearance of muscles between the left and right sides of the body, usually due to genetics rather than strength differences or imbalances
