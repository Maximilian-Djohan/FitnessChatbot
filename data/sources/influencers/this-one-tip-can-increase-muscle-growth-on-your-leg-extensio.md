# this one tip can increase muscle growth on your leg extension

This one tip can help increase your performance on the leg extension and increase muscle growth

A simple way to increase your performance on your leg extension is by maintaining dorsiflexion during your rep. What does this mean? Throughout the entirety of the rep have your toes faced inwards towards you. But what does this do?

The concept is the same as clenching your hand with a bluetooth handle. Using the concept of Sherrington's law of irradiation by contracting the muscle responsible for dorsiflexion like your tibialis you can better help recruit your quad and increase stability. So point your toes up during your leg extension.

Sherrington's Law of Irradiation, also known as the irradiation principle, describes how a contracting muscle can induce a neurological overflow to surrounding muscles, increasing their activation and potentially enhancing strength and stability
