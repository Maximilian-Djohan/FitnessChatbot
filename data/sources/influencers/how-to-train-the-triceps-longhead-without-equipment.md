# How to train the triceps longhead without equipment

How to train the triceps longhead without equipment

we know that to train the triceps longhead we want to perform elbow extension with upper arm by your side. but what if you dont have access to cables? what exercise can you do to train the triceps longhead?

there are two ways to do this. the first way is where you have absolutely no equipment. in this case you can do a pushdown isometric against an immoveable object with your arm at 90 degrees. we know that doing isometrics will grow muscle as you can have a high degree of effort and mechanical tension during them.

or if you have access to a dumbbell you can do a dumbbell pullover. while this might seem odd it will train the longheads function of shoulder extension where it has better leverage over the lats.
