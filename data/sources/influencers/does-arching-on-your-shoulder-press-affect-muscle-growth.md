# does arching on your shoulder press affect muscle growth?

does arching on your shoulder press affect muscle growth? can arching decrease muscle growth?

some people arch when they shoulder press but how does this affect your shoulder press? why might arching your back feel better on your shoulder press and is it better for muscle growth?

as you arch your arm path shifts away from the frontal plane causing adduction/flexion causing chest involvement as you are more internally rotated. another reason people find this comfortable is as it might make you go forward in the seat making you start at a higher degree of shoulder elevation and making it easier as youre taking out some of the bottom degrees of rom.

so what should you do? try to stay in the frontal plane. its ok to have an slight arch as long as its standardized but being in the frontal plane will be better for the delts. if the start of the press starts to feel awkward after this lower the seat so you start higher in the rep where its more natural/comfortable to be externally rotated.
