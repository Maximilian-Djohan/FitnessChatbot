# how high to go on your lat pulldown

How high do you need to go on your lat pulldown?

many people ask how high you should be going on your lat pulldown. they ask this as the higher you go up on your pulldown your lats contribute less compared to other muscles in those higher degrees of shoulder elevation. so what should you do?

well just by utilizing a wider grip this will be solved. the wider grip itself will limit the top of the rom so you will be safe to lock out your elbows. so use a grip 1.5x shoulder width and you can lock out your elbows on every rep.
