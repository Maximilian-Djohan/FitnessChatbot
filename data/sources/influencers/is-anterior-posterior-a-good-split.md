# is anterior posterior a good split?

Is Anterior Posterior a better split than Upper Lower? What is the best two day split?

Anterior Posterior is the front/back split. its structured with the muscles on the front of your upper and lower body one day then the backside the next day. most people program this as push + quads and pull + hams/glutes/adductors. but is this better than upper lower?

An anterior-posterior (AP) split is a resistance training program that divides exercises into two groups: anterior (front) and posterior (back) muscle groups.

lets look at the benefits of AP compared to UL. throughout both days you can either have both upper body exercises or lower body exercises first in your session unlike UL. this means if you have an imbalance or want to prioritize an entire half of your body you can do this better on AP.
