# What is the best tricep exercise?

What is the BEST tricep exercise?

for the triceps there are 3 exercises to maximize triceps growth. a long head biased exercise, medial/lateral head biased exercise and a proximal long head biased exercise. we will do this by disadvantaging the heads that we arent wanting to bias and advantaging the head we are trying to bias through exercise setup. you can do this through wrist position, shoulder angle and resistance profile

for the longhead biased exercise we need to disadvantage the other two heads. it will be a pushdown (with a fixed upper arm). we want a supinated grip, shoulder by our side and a descending resistance profile (hardest at start). what does this look like?

this can be done with a pushdown which matches the wrist position, shoulder angle and resistance profile. this is also done with a cuff to take any tension from the wrist.

for the medial/lateral head biased exercise we will disadvantage the long head. we will do this with an overhead extension. we will use a neutral grip and the most important thing is the shoulder angle being overhead where the longhead struggles to contribute. you can also make it ascending if youd like (but usually this makes it awkward).

this can be done through an overhead extension which matches the wrist position and shoulder angle. this is also done with a cuff to take any tension from the wrist.

for the next exercise we will disregard the elbow extension function of the triceps and train the shoulder extension function of the long head. (distal growth closer to the shoulder). the longhead good leverage above 90 degrees of shoulder extension over other muscles that also perform shoulder extension (lats).

Free weight (EZ bar) pullover exercise involves greater pectoralis major and triceps brachii (long head) muscle activation, compared to latissimus dorsi muscle activation

this is done through a top rom pullover stopping at 90 degrees (descending rp).
