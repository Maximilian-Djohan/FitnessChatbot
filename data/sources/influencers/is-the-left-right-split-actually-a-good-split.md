# Is the left right split actually a good split?

Is the left right split actually a good split?

the left right split is often joked about but can it actually be viable? it would have the same frequency as something like upper lower which is great. it will also be exclusively unilateral movements which we know will increase motor unit recruitment as we are only training one side at a time. so is it good?

Increasing motor unit recruitment during exercise is desirable because it allows for greater muscle force production and can lead to improved strength and hypertrophy. By recruiting more motor units, a larger number of muscle fibers are activated

it can be used if maybe an entire side of your body is lacking but realistically any other split that has 2x frequency like upper lower or 3x like full body will be better. but if you enjoy it (you don't) you can structure it like full body but split sides between two days.

(also whatever side you do 2nd will suffer due to cns fatigue from the day before)
