Excitation-secretion coupling failure (ESC failure) refers to a disruption in the process where an electrical signal triggers the release of substances like neurotransmitters or hormones from cells.

stretching also doesn't really help with soreness reduction. it will have a slight impact short term but nothing actually beneficial.

Some studies suggest that stretching, especially dynamic stretches, can improve blood flow and potentially ease soreness. However, other research indicates that stretching, either before or after exercise, does not significantly reduce delayed-onset muscle soreness (DOMS). 2
