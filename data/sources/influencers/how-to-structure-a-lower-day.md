# How to structure a lower day

How to "optimally" structure an entire leg/lower day.

first we want to identify what muscles we want to train. then we train their main function we do this by training their primary joint action.

PRIMARY MUSCLE GROUP JOINT ACTIONS
Quads: Knee extension
Hamstrings: Hip extension, Knee flexion
Add/abductors: Adduction/abduction
Glutes: Hip extension
Calf: Plantar flexion

starting off with quads we will train their function of knee extension. you can train this through a leg extension.

the hamstrings have the functions of knee flexion and hip extension. we can train this through a hamstring curl and hyperextension.

Your ad/abductors have the function of ab/adduction. which you will train through an hip ad/abduction machine.

the glutes have the function of hip extension. you can train this through a 90 degree extension or hip thrust.

the calves have the function of plantar flexion. you can train this through a straight leg calf raise.

perform these exercises in a lower rep range from 4-8 and for sets do what you can progress from. your sets per session will depend on your frequency of leg days.
