# What is the Best Range of Motion for the Side Delts?

What is the best range of motion for the side delt?

when doing a lateral raise variation (performing function of shoulder abduction) you want to start with your arms by your side up until 90 degrees.

why do we train in that range? the side delt has better relative leverage compared to the front delt up until that 90 degree range where the front delt will then take over

The internal moment arm lengths of the shoulder abductors change relative to one another with increasing shoulder elevation angle

using this information you can see that you would also be able to train your side delt in a shoulder press as well as long as your arms are below that 90 degree range

(if side delt isn't a priority/don't like lateral raises)
