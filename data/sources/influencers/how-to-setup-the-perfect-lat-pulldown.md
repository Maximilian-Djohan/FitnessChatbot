# How to setup the perfect lat pulldown?

How to setup the perfect lat pulldown. How wide should you go and how much ROM should you have?

when performing a lat pulldown you are training the lats function of shoulder adduction. many people say that when you go wider its better for the lats. what is actually the best setup?

grip width will vary from person to person depending on wingspan. you should hold around 1.5x-2x shoulder width but why is this? a wider grip will decrease the amount of shoulder elevation by cutting off part of the top rom. you do this as the lats lose leverage in that range to muscles like the chest.

now how far should you bring the bar down? your rep should end around 60-70 degrees where the lats have best leverage. any lower and they start to lose leverage again. what does this look like? it will depend from person to person but the bar/your hands should be around face level ranging from your forehead to chin.

So as a quick summary. many people like to emphasize the rom going super high and super low all the way to their chest. this isnt needed and isnt beneficial. stay in a rom where your lats can get trained well.
