# Pull-ups vs. Pulldowns What is best for muscle growth?

Pullups vs Pulldowns. What is the best for muscle growth?

there are often three points brought up when comparing the two exercises. what muscles the exercise targets, stability of the exercise and progressability. when performing either exercise you are performing shoulder adduction so they both target your lats (lower bias) and are both good exercises for this. but what about the other two points?

they have both different areas of stability. a pullup will be more stable throughout the upper portion of your body while a pulldown will be more stable towards the bottom as you have the leg pad. but overall the pulldown is more stable as you dont need to rely on other musculature to keep yourself stable.

for progressability the pulldown and pullup will be similar as you can add 2.5/5 pound jumps in weight. the only downside is that pullups can be awkward as it will be annoying to setup getting up to the bar and perform the reps with all of the weight in-between your legs.
