# does sweating mean you're having a good workout?

Does sweating mean youre having a good workout? Can sweating actually decrease muscle growth?

well what causes you to sweat during your workout? physical activity will increase your heart rate and raise your bodies temperature. as this continues your body will start to sweat to regulate this. but what does this have to do with muscle growth?
Sweating during a workout primarily means your body is cooling itself down.

It's a natural physiological response to increased body temperature, especially during physical activity.

sweating/being hot will cause uncomfortable sensations. why does this matter? these uncomfortable sensations will increase your perception of effort. and this increase in your perception of effort will result in less motor unit recruitment which will decrease muscle growth.

Motor unit recruitment is a measure of how many motor neurons are activated in a particular muscle, and therefore is a measure of how many muscle fibers of that muscle are activated. The higher the recruitment the stronger the muscle contraction will be.
