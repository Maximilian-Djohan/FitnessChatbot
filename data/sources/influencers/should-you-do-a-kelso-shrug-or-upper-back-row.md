# Should you do a kelso shrug or upper back row?

Should you do a Kelso Shrug or an Upper Back Row for upper back? Are your rear delts a limiting factor?

To train your upper back muscles (traps/rhomboids) you want to train their main function being scapular retraction. we know that in both exercises scapular retraction is present. but is one exercise better than another?

similar to how a fly is better than pressing for the chest. a kelso is better than a row for the upper back. while you will still effectively train the upper back as there is shoulder abduction (rear delts function) present your rear delt will impact the movement. this involvement will limit you but how much is person to person dependent. (due to individual leverages, weak/strong points and technique)

single joint > multi joint

a kelso can be used if you want to maximize your upper back growth but a wide grip row will still be great for it.
