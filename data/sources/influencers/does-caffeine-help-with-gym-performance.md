# Does caffeine help with gym performance?

Does caffeine actually help with gym performance?

many people consume caffeine before their workout through something like an energy drink or preworkout. but do they actually help with gym performance?

taking caffeine will actually help boost your performance in the gym. but how does it do this? taking caffeine will increase your maximum tolerable perceived effort which will increase motor unit recruitment and boost your gym performance.

Muscular failure is reached when the maximum tolerable level of perceived effort is reached, since this prevents further increases in effort, and therefore stops further increases in the central motor command that lead to further motor unit recruitment. In this way, afferent feedback limits motor unit recruitment levels at muscular failure.
