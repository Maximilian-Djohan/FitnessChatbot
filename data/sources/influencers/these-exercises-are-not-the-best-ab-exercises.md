# These exercises are not the best ab exercises

These exercises are NOT ab exercises they are actually quad/leg exercises

The way some ab "crunch" machines and hanging leg raises are performed make it so you are actually training your leg muscles. This is as though the movement you are pushing forward at the hips through hip flexion training your quad/hip flexors rather than spinal flexion (abs function). So how can you actually train the abs?

So when performing your ab exercise you need to actually make sure you're going through spinal flexion where you are actually bending at the spine.
