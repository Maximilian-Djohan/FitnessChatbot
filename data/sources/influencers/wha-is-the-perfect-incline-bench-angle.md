# What is the perfect incline bench angle?

What is the perfect incline dumbbell / barbell bench degree. what is the main purpose of the bench degree?

many people offer varying recommendations for the incline of which your bench should be. but to understand what incline you need to use you should know the purpose of it. the purpose of the bench incline is to determine where the exercise will be hardest. so how does incline affect this and what is the best incline?

during your press the hardest point will be where the line of force is farthest away from the joint. we want this to be around the middle portion of the rom to bias the upper chest. so to do so we will choose 30-45 degrees to allow for this.

flat = harder at start incline = harder towards middle
