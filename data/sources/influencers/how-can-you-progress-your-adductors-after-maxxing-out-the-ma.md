# How can you progress your adductors after maxxing out the machine

How can you progress your adductors after maxing out the machine?

training your adductors is important as it contributes a lot towards your leg width but many people max out the adduction machine relatively quickly. so what can you do to keep progressing?

the first and obvious thing to do is use a gym pin to increase the amount of load you can add on the machine (code bigpro427). but WE are broke so what can you do besides this?

you can increase the moment arm. what does this mean? you can move yourself back so only the edge of your knee is on the pad. having a longer moment arm will make the movement harder. another example of changing a moment arm can be seen when cuffed lateral raises at the shoulder vs at the wrist. the wrist will be harder because the moment arm is longer.

another way to make it harder is by adding more rom to progress by starting farther out.

last way to progress with an infinite progression ceiling are isometrics. you can do a three rep sets of 3 second max effort contractions. youll progress by producing more force during your max effort contraction. the only problem with this is that you wont be able to tell how much force is being produced but its still a viable option.

you can do a combination of these things or just do one or just not train adductors at all its up to you
