# Why did TNF stop doing flys?

Why did TNF stop doing presses for flys? Should you make the switch?

TNF switched to flys to bring up his chest. He is using flys to do so as you will only be performing the functions of the chest being shoulder adduction and shoulder flexion compared to something like pressing where you have elbow extension where your triceps will contribute.

so should you switch to flys instead of pressing? it depends on your goals. yes if you want to prioritize your chest further then it will be beneficial to make the switch. this isn't to say that pressing won't grow your chest. pressing for your chest will still be great.
