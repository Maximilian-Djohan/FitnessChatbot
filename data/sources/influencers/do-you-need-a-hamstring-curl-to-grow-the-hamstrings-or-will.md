# Do you need a hamstring curl to grow the hamstrings or will a hinge be enough?

Do you need a hamstring curl to grow your hamstrings? Is a hinge sufficient for hamstring growth?

we know that the hamstring has the functions of knee flexion as well as hip extension. so doing a hinge will train the hamstring as it trains hip extension. but do you even need a hamstring curl then?

yes you need a hamstring curl to efficiently grow your hamstring. the hamstring is made up of four muscles and while they all cross the knee to perform knee flexion not all of them cross the hip. the biceps femoris short head doesnt cross the hip which means it WONT be trained in a hinge.

Semitendinosus, Biceps femoris (long head)

Semimembranosus

Biceps femoris (short head)

so if you want to effectively train the entire hamstring you want to include a knee flexion exercise such as a hamstring curl. but yes you can still grow your hamstring without one
