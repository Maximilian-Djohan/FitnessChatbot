# Should you be training your erectors?

Should you be training your erectors? What is the best spinal erector exercise?

erectors are an important part of your physique. though it is a slight misconception that they are columns of muscle that you see on your back. in actuality what you see is your lats which are being pushed outward by your erectors making your back look larger. so how should you train your erectors?

Traps (Rhomboids Underneath) Spinal Erectors

most people indirectly train their erectors through a hinge like a deadlift variation or hyper extension. but this actually isnt the best way as in these exercises you are training hip extension not the erectors function of spinal extension. in a hinge like this they are just playing a stabilizing role making sure your back doesnt bend during it (this will still be great/enough for erector stimulus)

hip extension vs spinal extension

but if you want to maximize your erector growth you should try to isolate spinal extension. what exercise is good for this? you can set up a normal hyper extension but instead of you bending at the hips (hip extension) you place the pad farther up (above hips) so when you bend you are only going into spinal flexion and extension.
