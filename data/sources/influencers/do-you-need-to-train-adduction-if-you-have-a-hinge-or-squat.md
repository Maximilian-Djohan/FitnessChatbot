# Do you need to train adduction if you have a hinge or squat?

Do you need to train adduction if you already have a squat/hinge?

many people dont directly train their adductors through adduction and rely on a squat/hinge to train it through hip extension. but we know that adductors are important as they can contribute around 1/3 of your leg width. so can you effectively train them with a squat/hinge?

no you cant. there are five muscles that make up the adductors and only the adductor magnus will be effectively trained in a hip extension exercise. and while it is the most important part to train you will still be missing out.

The main function of both the proximal and distal regions of the adductor magnus muscle is to perform hip extension.

so no to effectively train the adductors you want both an adduction and hip extension to effectively train the adductors.
