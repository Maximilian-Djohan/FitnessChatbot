# does squatting train your adductors?

Does squatting train your adductors? Does foot placement affect this?

some people say that your adductors will be fully trained in a squat pattern. people also say that using something like a wider foot stance can affect this but is this true?

the adductors is made up of multiple different muscles. in a squat where there is hip extension only the adductor magnus is trained. so an adduction exercise is needed if you want to train the entirety of the adductor. a wider foot placement wont change this as adduction isnt being loaded. the only thing that can change in a squat is depth as more hip extension you are performing will involve more adductor magnus.

so what does this mean? no a squat is not enough to train the entirety of your adductors and you will need an adduction exercise alongside a hip extension exercise to train everything.
