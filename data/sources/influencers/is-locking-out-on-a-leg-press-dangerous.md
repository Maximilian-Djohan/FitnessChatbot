# is locking out on a leg press dangerous?

is locking out on a leg press dangerous?

its commonly thought that locking out can be harmful. most of this comes from a few popular videos online. also the idea of loading a lot of weight and locking it out can seem dangerous. but do these assumptions actually have any ground?

The question of whether or not to lock out on the leg press is a debated topic among fitness professionals.

in practice when using weight that is manageable to you and performing the exercise without momentum and good form it is completely safe. it is also better for the knee joint/tendons as you are able to strengthen them throughout the joint range of motion.

though there are some exceptions to this. if you are hypermobile there is the risk of hyperextending your knees. or if you are on PEDs your muscles can out develop tendons which can lead to injury. but as long as you are in control you will be fine.

Hypermobile joints refer to joints that move beyond the normal range of motion with little effort

so is locking out dangerous? no as long as you are using a manageable weight that you have progressed up to with good form/technique you will be good
