# What is good shoulder press form?

What is good shoulder press form? How should you setup your shoulder press for best muscle growth?

Starting off with seat height. The seat height will determine where you start the rep. Having the seat higher will allow you to go deeper in rom and recruit more side delt while having it lower will recruit more front delt. Whichever you choose will depend on your goal with the shoulder press.

Next is grip choice. If your goal is to train your delts over your upper pec you should pick the supinated grip as it will keep you in the frontal plane and make sure youre training pure abduction over any shoulder flexion. The width of the grip will determine how much tricep involvement you have the closer your grip the more your tricep is involved as you will be getting more elbow extension.

Another thing to note is how your body is orientated during the press the more arched you are the more it will take you out of the frontal plane and in turn add more shoulder flexion recruiting your upper pec.

So as a summary if your goal is to train your front delt press above 90 degrees, if you want side delt alongside it go lower, use the supinated grip, use a wider grip to limit triceps involvement, and stay upright during your press.
