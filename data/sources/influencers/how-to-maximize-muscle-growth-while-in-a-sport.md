# How to maximize muscle growth while in a sport

How to maximize muscle growth while having a sport/other activity?

while many people prioritize lifting but some people have an extra physical activity that they do. but how can you balance training in both areas while making it so they wont affect each other?

in this case you still want to maximize muscle growth so you want to train every muscle as frequent as you can training them 2-3x per week. also you want to have as many rest days as possible as your other physical activities will contribute muscle damage. this can be done with an Full Body 3x or Upper Lower 2x as you will still train all muscles frequently while having a good amount of rest days.

When strength-trained lifters perform workouts with moderate loads to failure, strength recovery is linearly determined by the number of sets performed. This has important implications for full body and upper-lower split routines.

We can probably recover from 3 sets 72 hours ago

so basically you want to make sure you have intelligent programming tracking volume, making sure you are training frequently, have an adequate amount of rest days. and make sure you can progress at both things.

(if you train for your physical activity having it timed after training would be the best but if you do it on your rest day that will be fine there will always be tradeoffs.)
