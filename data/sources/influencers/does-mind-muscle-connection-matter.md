# does mind muscle connection matter?

Does mind muscle connection matter? Do you need to "feel" a muscle in order for it to grow?

some people say they have the problem of "feeling" a given muscle during an exercise. this is often associated with lat training as many people struggle to feel their lats during a pulldown. is this an actual problem and what should you do if it is?

your main priority is to have perfect technique/form. if your form is perfect you will be targeting the muscle you are trying to train. in something like a pulldown you are trying to train the lats through shoulder adduction. if you are perfectly performing shoulder adduction you will be training the lats.

also as a disclaimer. another reason you could struggle to "feel" certain muscles in exercises is because of the rom you use. using a pulldown as an example when going too high up/low in shoulder adduction other muscles such as the chest will gain leverage and contribute.
