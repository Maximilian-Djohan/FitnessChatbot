# Can you waste noobie gains?

What are "noobie gains"? Can you waste them?

What are noobie gains? people often think its the time period around when you first start out where you have increased growth. this is not accurate. "noobie gains" arent a time frame its just a certain amount of starting muscle that you will develop faster.

Newbie gains, the initial rapid increase in muscle mass and strength experienced by beginners, typically last for 6 to 12 months.

so can they be wasted? no. that first amount of muscle will always be put on faster. essentially what it is when starting out you have a wider range of the motor unit pool to develop. and as you continue to train this will start to slow down overtime. this will make the growth at the start of lifting progress faster than later on

A motor unit is a single motor neuron and all the muscle fibers it innervates. A motor unit pool is a collection of all the motor units that innervate a single muscle. Essentially, it's the group of motor neurons and muscle fibers working together to control a specific muscle.
