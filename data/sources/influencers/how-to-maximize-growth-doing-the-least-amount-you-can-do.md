How to maximize muscle growth doing the least amount you can do?

(30min 2x a week)

To maximize muscle growth with minimal effort,

First is exercise selection where we want to choose compound exercises that work as many joint actions per exercise so we can train as many muscle groups as possible with the least amount of exercises.

PRIMARY MUSCLE GROUP JOINT ACTIONS

Knee flexion

Calf Plantar flexion

For the upper body you can do neutral grip press which will work all of the chest with some tricep/delt. Next is a close grip deep frontal plane press to work the front/side delt and tricep. and to finish off the upper body you can do a close grip row where you also shrug to train the lats, traps/rhomboids and rear delts.

for the lower body you can do a squat pattern which will work the quads, adductors and glutes. and to finish off the legs you can also do a hinge/sldl to work the hamstrings/adductors with a bit of glutes and erectors.

how should you program volume to spend the least amount of time in the gym? you might think going once a week will be the most time efficient but you would need to do 4 sets of each exercise to grow muscle training once a week which would add up to doing 20 sets. instead you can do 1 set twice a week with the total volume coming out to 5 sets twice a week which will be more time efficient.
