# Vertical shrugs are not the best to train the upper traps.

Vertical shrugs are NOT the best way to train your upper traps. What is the best exercise for your upper traps?

many people that want to train their upper traps and do so through shoulder elevation which makes sense looking at the motion visually but this is not best way. while shoulder elevation will train the upper traps it would be better to train it through shoulder abduction. but why is this?

the upper trap inserts onto the scapula which results in it being better trained through abduction taking it through a better motion. this is also seen in data where the upper traps have better activation in abduction over elevation.

so if you want to train your upper traps you should do a lat raise above 90 degrees.

While the upper trapezius is typically assumed to be most active during shrugs, It displays higher muscle activation during shoulder abduction. in contrast, the middle and lower trapezius display comparatively higher activation in shrugs.

one thing to keep in mind as you are training abduction above 90 degrees the front delt will be worked. but yes abduction > elevation for biasing the upper traps
