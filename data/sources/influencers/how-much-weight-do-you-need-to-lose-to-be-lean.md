# how much weight do you need to lose to be lean?

How much weight do you need to lose to be lean? (you need to lose a lot more than you think)

many people when cutting down to 10-15% body fat very often overestimate their muscle mass and underestimate how much fat they have/how much they need to lose. they will end up saying something like "i just need to lose 10-15 pounds" when most people wont be able to accurately estimate these values and end up needing to lose 30-40 pounds.

as an example going from 30% to 10% body fat when starting at 175lbs. at 175lbs you will have ~52lbs of fat and to go down to 10% bodyfat you will need to lose ~39lbs of that fat. which means you will be going from 175lbs to ~135lbs.

so when cutting down weight to achieve a certain look while you can have weight goals you shouldnt hyper fixate on bodyfat percentage and the weight youre at. instead you should be just focus on visually getting to the point you want to get to and be open to losing more than you think you will need to lose.
