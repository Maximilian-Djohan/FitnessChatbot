# Adductor Muscle Training Explained

Your Adductors (inner thigh) is made up of 5 muscles: the adductor magnus, longus, brevis, pectineus and gracilis.

Your adductors main function is hip adduction (bringing your thigh inward).

You can train hip adduction on the adduction machine.

But the adductor magnus (biggest part of your adductor) also has the function of hip extension.

Hip extension can be trained through a Romanian deadlift/stiff leg deadlift.
