# what's is intra workout?

What is intraworkout? Should you take it and can it help increase muscle growth?

intraworkout is something you drink during your workout to maintain blood glucose levels/reduce intrasession fatigue and will help increase performance. if you workout for longer then 60 minutes this can be something that could help you. but what should you take?

starting off with the most important thing being the carb source. you want around 60-80g of carbs. then alongside this it can be beneficial to add in your electrolytes and optionally a protein source through essential amino acids/whey. this should all be in around ~1l of water. the main thing from your intra will be your carbs.

| Category | More "Expensive" Option | Cheaper Option | No Supplement Option |
|---|---|---|---|
| Carb | Cluster Dextrin (digests faster) | Gatorade Powder | Orange Juice |
| Electrolyte | Ekkolyte | 1/2 tsp Salt + 1/2 tsp NoSalt | Optional Salt / NoSalt |
| Protein | EAAs / Unflavored Whey | - | - |

so if you have longer sessions or don't have a lot of pre workout carbs intra can be useful. and no something like intraworkout gummies won't be good as they won't digest fast enough.
