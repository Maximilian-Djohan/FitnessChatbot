# Is training the brachioradialis important?

Is training the brachioradialis important? How do you train it?

The brachioradialis is one of the elbow flexors but it is on the forearm. it is also something that is important to train as it has a large cross-sectional area. so then how should you train it?

well as it flexes the elbow we will want to disadvantage the other elbow flexors such as the biceps and brachialis. so how do you do this? pick an exercise with an ascending resistance profile meaning it's hardest at the top. also take a pronated grip to further disadvantage the biceps. so what would this look like?

a great example of this is a cuffed reverse curl as it will be hardest around 90 degrees where the brachioradialis has best leverage. then if you want to take it a step further you can start the rep higher up to eliminate the bottom rom where the other elbow flexors contribute more
