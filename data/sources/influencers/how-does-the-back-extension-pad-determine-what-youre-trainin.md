# how does the back extension pad determine what you're training?

how does the back extension pad setup determine what muscle you are training?

in a back extension you can either isolate the erectors or train the hip extensors (alongside the erectors). this is determined by the pad height as it will determine how much spinal extension or hip extension you are working through. so how will the pad height affect this?

well its simple the higher up you place the pad the more the pad will be on your torso making you bend at the torso. this will allow for more spinal extension and in turn erector work. and if you place it lower you will instead be able to hinge at the hips training your hip extensors.
