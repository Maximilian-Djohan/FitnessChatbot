# What are the different types of pulldown/pullup variations?

What are the different types of
pulldown/pullup variations? Which
is the best for muscle growth?

there can be three functions that you are training

while doing these exercises. shoulder adduction -

lower lat bias, shoulder extension - upper lat bias,
and elbow flexion - biceps.

adduction extension flexion

iN fe &

when doing a wide grip pull you are primarily
challenging shoulder adduction - lower lats bias

when you are doing a neutral closer grip pull you are
primarily challenging shoulder extension. upper lat
bias.

when doing a closer reverse grip pull you are
challenging more shoulder extension alongside elbow
flexion. upper lat bias + biceps.

which is the best? wide grip for lower lat bias, close
grip for upper lat bias. and i wouldn’t recommend
doing a reverse pull as you should train biceps
separately. keep in mind that no matter what you
do all three functions can be performing to an
extent it just depends to what degree

(also "biasing" doesnt make a significant difference but you should train both lat functions)
