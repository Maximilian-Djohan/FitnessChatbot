# How to maintain or even progress muscle on vacation

How to maintain muscle and even progress on vacation without any equipment.

we know that muscle atrophy will occur when you arent training and many people stress about this during vacation so how can you maintain and even progress muscle without a gym?

When muscle fibers are not in a post-workout stimulus period, they display atrophy. Maintenance studies allow us to determine how fast this happens.

some people might say to do bodyweight stuff but even with that would be too easy for many people and you would be limited on what muscles you can train. so what should you do? isometrics are great in this scenario. you can contract your muscle with max effort against an object for every muscle group which will provide a high degree of effort and mechanical tension and produce muscle growth.

Voluntarily increasing central motor command increases perception of effort, and the number of recruited motor units (and the number of activated muscle fibers)

isometrics are great and you can perform exercises for every muscle group with them by mimicking the exercise pattern without any active movement just contracting against something. you can do multiple sets of 15 seconds with reps being bursts of 3-5 seconds. this would be enough to maintain or even grow muscle if done correctly
