# The front raise is not a front delt exercise.

The front raise is NOT a front delt exercise

the front raise is often thought of as being a front delt exercise. when looking at the movement we can tell that we are training shoulder flexion. what muscles perform shoulder flexion is it a front delt exercise?

what we are training depends what part of the rom we do. but in a majority of shoulder flexion from 20-120 degrees it will be an upper chest exercise. we can determine this by seeing how the upper chest has better relative leverage in this range.

but you can still make it a front delt exercise by limiting the rom. from 0-20 degrees it will have better leverage compared to the upper chest so you can perform a bottom rom front raise
