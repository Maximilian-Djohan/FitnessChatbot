# how does grip width affect your pressing?

how does grip width affect your pressing? is one better for muscle growth?

we know that when performing a press there are three things that are happening: horizontal adduction, elbow extension and shoulder flexion. how does grip affect this?

horizontal adduction elbow extension shoulder flexion

when using a closer grip you will have more elbow extension making you use more triceps while when using a wider grip you will have more horizontal adduction which makes it more of a chest focus.

elbow extension

but what if you want to train your upper pec how do you get more shoulder flexion? first you want to take a closer grip to make your arms more tucked. but how do you limit tricep involvement when you have a closer grip? you need to make sure your arms are in front of you (move bench back) so your wrists are stacked upon your elbows as you press to limit elbow extension.

(arm path matters more than bench incline)
(you want your arms a bit closer than the pic and bench bit farther back)

so is one better? no. it depends what your goal is. wider = more mid/lower pec, closer = more tricep, closer + wrist/elbow stacked = upper pec
