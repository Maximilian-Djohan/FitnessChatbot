Strength training volume can probably be best measured by recording the number of reps done with full motor unit recruitment and at slow bar speeds.

This analysis assumes that full motor unit recruitment is reached on each rep of a SRM load (85–87% of 1RM). This number (5 reps) may differ between individuals and also between muscle groups, heavy loads, extra sets need to be done to compensate for the lower number of stimulating reps per set.
