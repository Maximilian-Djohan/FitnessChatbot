# Is the JM press vaulted?

Is the JM Press vaulted? Is it actually good for the tricep?

the JM Press is a popular tricep exercise but is it overrated or is it actually a good exercise? we know that the tricep performs elbow extension which the JM Press trains so is it good?

the JM Press is a good medial and lateral head exercise. all heads are trained through elbow extension but as there will be shoulder flexion occurring the longhead being biarticular crossing the shoulder and elbow joint will make it suffer from antagonistic inhibition making it not get trained to any good degree.

Antagonistic inhibition, in the context of muscle function, refers to the natural process where the nervous system inhibits the activity of a muscle (the antagonist) when its opposing muscle (the agonist) contracts to produce movement.

something to keep in mind is that there will be a degree of shoulder and chest involvement as there will be some shoulder flexion and horizontal flexion present. so if compared to something like a overhead extension which is also a medial/lateral head exercise it will be better as there won't be any other muscles involved
