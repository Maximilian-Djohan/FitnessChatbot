# should you be doing your leg extension before your squat pattern?

Should you be doing your leg extension before your squat pattern for most muscle growth?

many people perform their squat pattern before their leg extension but doing it the other way around will be better for the quads. the first reason being that squat pattern being more lengthened focused which will be more fatiguing than the leg extension. so doing it first will have more impact on later exercises. so doing leg extensions first will allow you to get more out of them.

another reason why you would better train the quads having your leg extension is because of the size of them. we know that when we are less fatigued we will be recruiting the most amount of muscle fibers. this is the most important for muscles like the quads as they suffer from the voluntary activation deficit meaning they struggle being recruited due to their size. so training them where there are less muscles active when they arent fatigued will allow you to grow them the best.

Larger muscles display lower voluntary activation levels than smaller muscles.

the exception to this would be if youre doing a free weight squat. while you will suffer from the recruitment/fatigue factors on the leg extension it is important to do the squat pattern first as the free weight variant will have a high coordination demand. so by performing the squat pattern first you will be able to perform better on it.

so what should you do? do leg extensions first if you do a squat pattern. but if you do a squat pattern that has a higher coordination demand you can do that first.
