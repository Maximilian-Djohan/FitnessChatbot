# how to maximize adductor growth?

how to MAXIMIZE muscle growth in the adductors

your adductor magnus is a large muscle on the inner thigh that is a main contributor to leg width. to train it you want to train its two functions of hip extension and hip adduction

to train hip adduction which will all parts of the adductor all you will need is the adductor machine which will be a majority of your adductor growth

for hip extension you can do a hinge like a SLDL/RDL which will train the adductor magnus (biggest part of the adductor) closer to the hip then your adduction machine.

so to maximize growth you want to do a adduction and a hinge. you will train the entirety of the adductor in the adduction but you will get more proximal growth doing a hinge.
