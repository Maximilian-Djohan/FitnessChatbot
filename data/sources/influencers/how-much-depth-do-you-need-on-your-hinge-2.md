# How much depth do you need on your hinge?

How much depth do you need on your hinge? How does it affect muscle growth of different muscles?

first we need to determine what you are trying to train with your hinge pattern. this is simple with your hinge pattern you are training hip extension. but how does depth affect what muscle gets worked how far should you be going?

Anatomical position Extension of the hip joint

we will be using an SLDL as example as it is a standard hip hinge with a full rom. the SLDL has a descending resistance profile as it will be hardest towards the bottom. your three muscles (ham,glute,adductor) responsible for hip extension also have different leverages throughout this rom.

Towards the top of the rep the glutes will have best leverage, towards the middle your hamstrings will have best leverage and towards the bottom near the floor your adductor magnus will have best leverage with your erectors also contributing more as you will have more spine stabilization demand.

Proportional contribution of the hip extensor muscles alters with joint angle?

so if your goal is hamstrings you should go around halfway for hamstring biased curl or all the way if you want more adductors and erectors.
