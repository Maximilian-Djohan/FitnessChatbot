# Can you gain muscle while being in a deficit

Can you gain muscle while cutting weight/being in a calorie deficit?

its a common thought that you cant build muscle while losing weight. this isnt actually true. you can 100% build muscle while losing weight with a good diet and training whether your are a beginner or advanced.

Muscle hypertrophy with large-scale weight loss and resistance training

JE Donnelly, T Sharp, J Houmard, M G Carlson, J O Hill, J E Whatley, R G Israel

PMID: 8379514 DOI: 10.1093/ajcn/58.4.561

no matter the circumstance you will build muscle while in a deficit if your diet and training are good. you wont be able to maximize growth if you are not eating at maintenance but you can get very close if you eat an adequate amount of protein and an adequate amount of carbs in which many people disregard when cutting as they fixate on total calories.

(yes as an advanced trainee it will be harder for you to gain muscle while in a deficit but thats mostly from the fact that its just harder to gain muscle when youre advanced.
