# Do you need a leg extension to grow the quads?

Do you need a leg extension to grow the quads? Is a squat pattern sufficient for quad growth?

we know that the quads have the function of knee extension. a squat pattern will train knee extension so do you even need a leg extension?

yes you do need a leg extension. as the rectus femoris is a biarticular joint that crosses both the hip and the knee it will suffer from antagonistic inhibition in a squat where you are changing the angle of your knee and hip you wont be training it effectively. so if you want to train the rectus femoris you want to have a leg extension.

so yes while you can grow the majority of the quads just from a squat pattern you will be missing out on quad growth not having a leg extension.
