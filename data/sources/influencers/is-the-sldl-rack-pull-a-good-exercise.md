# Is the SLDL rack pull a good exercise?

Is the SLDL rack pull a good

we know that we should do a hinge for the hamstrings and many people do either a 45 degree back extension or SLDL for this. but would something like a rack pull be better?

well what is different between a rack pull and normal sldl? you are cutting off the bottom part of ROM. in a SLDL it gets harder the lower you go and in turn works the adductor magnus more so when you limit the rom around halfway it will be hardest where the hamstrings have better leverage similar to a 45 degree back extension.

so a rack pull can be a great setup to train your hamstrings through hip extension and will be better than an SLDL for the hamstrings as you are limiting the adductor magnus.
