# how much does muscle contribute to your maintenance?

How much does muscle actually contribute to your maintenance calories? How much more can you eat if youre muscular?

many people say that one of the reasons you should build more muscle is because youll be able to eat more as you have a higher TDEE. this fact is also reinforced when you see bodybuilders eat as they are able to eat much more than the average person. so how much does muscle actually contribute to your maintenance?

well theres two ways to look at this. the resting rate calorie increases just from living with more muscle and the active increase during activity. starting off with the resting rate increase which is much lower than you think. the average rate per 1 pound of muscle is 6-13 calories. this means for an extra 40 pounds (around most peoples "natural limit" with many years of training) will only increase your maintenance by 240-520 calories
