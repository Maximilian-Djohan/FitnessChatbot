# the best hip extensor exercises for every muscle

the BEST hip extension exercises for all of the hip extensors: hamstrings, glutes and adductor magnus

There are three main muscle groups that perform hip extension which you should be training. but if they all perform hip extension what is the best exercise you should choose for each of them?

for hamstrings you want to choose a hinge that is hardest in the mid range a 45 degree back extension is great for this. (a sldl will also be great but i wouldnt go too low)

for the glutes you want a shortened position movement a 90 degree back extension or a hipthrust will be great for this.

for the adductor magnus it will be best trained in deep hip extension like going far down on your SLDL

so what exercises should you have? i would pick one hinge with a straight knee which will effectively train the hamstring and adductor magnus and a shortened position exercise for the glutes.
