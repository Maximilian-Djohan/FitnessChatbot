# The leg raise is NOT an ab exercise

The leg raise is NOT an ab exercise

we know to train the abs you want to perform trunk flexion. the problem with a leg raise is that most people are just flexing the hip which trains other muscles like the rectus femoris of the quad. while you can adjust it to make sure you are bending the spine after your legs are up most of the rom you will just be doing hip flexion.

you cannot bias portions of the ab as they are all connected so when you perform an exercise the entire muscle will contract. so instead you should just pick a stable crunch variation that is easy stable and easy to progress.
