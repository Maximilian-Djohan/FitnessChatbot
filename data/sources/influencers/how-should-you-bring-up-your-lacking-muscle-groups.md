# How should you bring up your lacking muscle groups?

how should you bring up your lacking muscle groups?

if you have a lacking muscle group there are four main things you need to make sure you are doing to bring it up. train it with higher frequency, do single joint exercises, do unilateral movements, manage volume and have it at the start of your session. but why do these things help?

first we know that training a muscle more frequently is better then doing more volume less frequently. next you can utilize single joint exercises to make sure you have motor unit recruitment to the given muscle (also no limiting muscles). unilateral movement will also increase motor unit recruitment towards the given muscle. managing your volume by doing your maximum recoverable volume but not being too excessive as it could harm you. and prioritizing the muscle by putting it at the start of your session so you are fresh for it.
