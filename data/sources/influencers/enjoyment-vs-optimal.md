# Enjoyment vs Optimal

Enjoyment versus being Optimal which is more important? Can enjoyment overcome optimization?

enjoyment is important in training as it will correlate with motivation. doing an exercise you dont will cause you to have less motivation. with less motivation you will have a higher perception of effort which will decrease motor unit recruitment. this will in turn lead to less muscle growth. so does enjoyment matter more than optimization?

Motor unit recruitment is a measure of how many motor neurons are activated in a particular muscle, and therefore is a measure of how many muscle fibers of that muscle are activated. The higher the recruitment the stronger the muscle contraction will be.

in some cases it can be argued that enjoyment is better. when comparing something like a bench press to a machine press. the main difference is just stability so it can be argued that if you truly hate the machine pressing it can be beneficial for you to bench.

in some cases enjoyment will not outweigh the optimization. if you were to compare something like a bench press to a machine fly. there is no amount of enjoyment that will take your triceps out of your bench press. in something like this a fly it will always be better as its single joint.

so what should you do? do what you want. if you want to be optimal then be optimal. if you want to do something you enjoy then do that. but if you do something you enjoy in a majority of cases it wont be more optimal than something else but that is fine not everything has to be optimal for you to do it.
