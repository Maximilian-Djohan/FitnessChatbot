# Common form mistakes on a lat biased row

Common form mistakes during your lat row that can decrease muscle growth

we know that in a lat row our goal is to train the lats through its function of shoulder extension. but what are things we should look out for during our set?

shoulder extension:

the first issue that many people have is protraction of the scapula letting their shoulder blades fall forward. this will make you retract and train the upper back and instead you should try to avoid protraction. another thing to avoid is hinging at the hips as many people go into hip extension to cheat up the weight or hinge forward thinking they are stretching the lats which isnt the case.

next is your arm path during your rep this will determine your biceps involvement. you want your wrist and elbow to be stacked with the resistance. to allow for this you will need to change your seat height which for most people will be higher up.

so the things you should be looking for is keeping posture in your torso avoiding any unnecessary movement and your armpath to limit biceps involvement.
