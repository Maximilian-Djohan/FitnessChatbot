# Is the upright row a good shoulder exercise?

Is the upright row a good shoulder exercise?

the upright row is often seen as an old school body builder exercise. it is promoted as a good shoulder exercise over a traditional exercise like a lateral raise. is this true does it have any benefit over other shoulder exercises?

similar to a lateral raise you are training the function of shoulder abduction. it is the same arm path just with a bent elbow. but having a bent elbow and bent wrists will put unnecessary strain on them give discomfort making shoulder abduction worse. a bent elbow is also actually shown to have worse activation.
(this only has effect if the load is below the elbow as you aren't loading your upper arm well)

Middle deltoid activation is lower when the lateral raise is performed with a bent

so when trying to grow shoulders you should just pick a stable variation. (it's ok to have a bent elbow if your upper arm is effectively being loaded. rather than your forearm being loaded making abduction uncomfortable.)
