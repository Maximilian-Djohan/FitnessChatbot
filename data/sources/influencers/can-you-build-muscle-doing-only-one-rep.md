Can you build muscle only doing 1 rep?

many people say that you cant build muscle on rep and it will be only strength but can you actually build muscle doing one rep?

What is the 1 rep max?

One-repetition maximum (one-rep max or 1RM) in weight training is the maximum amount of weight that a person can possibly lift for one repetition. It may also be

one rep to failure WILL provide stimulus towards muscle growth. but you will be missing out. as we know ~5 reps to failure will be the most stimulating you will be missing out on stimulating reps and in turn potential growth. so you should be aiming to get at least ~5 reps if your goal is to build muscle.

Strength training volume can probably be best measured by recording the number of reps done with full motor unit recruitment

but if you want to test your 1 rep max or are trying to improve your strength using reps lower than 5 will have its uses.
