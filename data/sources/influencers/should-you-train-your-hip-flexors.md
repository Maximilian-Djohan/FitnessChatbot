# Should you train your hip flexors?

Should you train your hip flexors and can it help maximize quad growth? What muscles flex the hip?

The main muscles that contribute to hip flexion are the Iliopsoas muscles, Sartorius and the Rectus femoris of the quad. but how can you train hip flexion?

Psoas major, Iliac crest, Iliacus, Tensor fasciae latae, Pectineus, Adductor longus, Sartorius, Adductor magnus, Rectus femoris, Vastus lateralis, Gracilis, Vastus medialis, Patella

training the hip flexors is very simple you just want to flex the hip which can be done on something like a leg extension. but an important thing to keep in mind is to keep the angle of the knee joint the same throughout the entirety of the rep to not cause antagonistic inhibition in the rec fem.

you can train hip flexion if you like to "maximize" quad development by having more rectus femoris growth proximal to the hip and to train your other hip flexors. but overall its not something important and can be done as something extra if you want.
