# can digestion walks increase muscle growth?

Should you be doing digestion walks after eating? Can digestion walks increase muscle growth?

some people say that you NEED to be doing digestion walks after eating a meal. but does this actually have a purpose and can it even help with muscle growth?

well digestion walks can help with digestion and insulin sensitivity/nutrient partitioning which can help with your food being used towards muscle growth. this can be done by doing some low intensity cardio / walking after your meals for 10-20 minutes.

Digestion walks, or light post-meal strolls, improve nutrient uptake and blood sugar regulation, creating an optimal environment for muscle growth rather than direct muscle building.

but while this can help its importance is overstated. your insulin sensitivity will already be in a good state just from being at a healthy bodyfat percentage. and even then insulin sensitivity doesnt have a significant impact on muscle growth as seen comparing growth of people who are more insulin resistant vs sensitive. so this can be a cherry ontop to whatever you do but its not needed.
