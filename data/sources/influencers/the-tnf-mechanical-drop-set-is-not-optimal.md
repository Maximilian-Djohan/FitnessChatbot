# The TNF mechanical drop set is NOT optimal

The TNF mechanical dropset is NOT optimal for muscle growth

the TNF mechanical dropset is a tricep workout where you do three exercises back to back while dropping the weight.

but why is it not optimal? the fact that its in a dropset will be limiting you. after you reach failure on the first exercise by hitting your maximum tolerable perceived effort but still continuing will result in you performing with less effort. this is due to CNS fatigue which will in turn lead to not being able to recruit as many muscle fibers in the subsequent exercises in the dropset.

Supraspinal CNS fatigue refers to a decrease in the brain's ability to effectively recruit and activate motor neurons, leading to a reduction in muscle force output, even when the individual is trying their hardest.

so what should you do instead. well we know that the pushdown (longhead biased) and overhead extension (medial/lateral head biased) are great exercises so instead of performing them back to back in a dropset where you will be limited by cns fatigue you can instead just do them as separate straight sets with rest in between.
