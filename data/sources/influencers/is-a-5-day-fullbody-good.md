# is a 5 day fullbody good?

Is a 5 day fullbody actually a good split?

splits like full body 3x and full body eod will be great for muscle growth so would something like 5x full body be even better as you are increasing your frequency?

well this comes up with two points. CNS fatigue from previous sessions will impair subsequent sessions as their is no rest and since you have already trained those muscle groups prior they will still be in growth and wont benefit much from back to back training.

so basically just stick to a normal split. fb 3x or eod will be better than fb 5x and if you want to be in the gym more just run a two day split 6x like ULULULR.
