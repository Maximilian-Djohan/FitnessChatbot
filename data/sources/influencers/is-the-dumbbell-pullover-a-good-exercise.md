# Is the dumbbell pullover a good exercise

Is the dumbbell pullover a good exercise? Is this actually a good tricep exercise?

first we need to look at what's being trained in this dumbbell pullover. you are training shoulder extension. this is often associated with training the lats with something like a pullover or close grip row so would this even train the tricep at all?

shoulder extension:

we know the triceps perform elbow extension. but the longhead as it crosses the shoulder also performs shoulder extension. but how can we tell if this will train it? we can look at the leverages of muscles responsible for shoulder extension. the triceps will have good leverage over the lats while your arms are overhead in something like a db pullover so it will train the tricep.

Shoulder elevation angle affects the capacity of the triceps brachii long head to produce shoulder extension moment, likely by increasing its internal moment arm length.

Free weight (EZ bar) pull over exercise involves greater pectoralis major and triceps brachii (long head) muscle activation, compared to latissimus dorsi muscle activation.

so yes a db pullover or partial rom pullover when your arms are overhead will train the tricep's longhead as it has better leverage over the lats in that range.
