# how to fulfill your micronutrients

How to fulfill all of your Micronutrients
Pt. 1- Water Soluble Vitamins

starting off with water soluble vitamins this will be your B and C vitamins. these are vitamins that you can eat individually as they are water soluble and dont need outside help for absorption. though something to consider is pairing your vitamin C with Iron to boost its absorption. but besides that lets get into food choices

Starting with the B vitamins a mix food choices such as oats, brown rice, legumes (lentils/beans), beef liver, egg yolks will work together to fulfill this. but another choice to keep in mind is fortified foods such as a fortified nutritional yeast where two table spoons can cover a majority of your B micros. as well as fortified cereals can be good as well. with all of these sources having good bioavailability.

next is vitamin C which is very easy to hit. commonly this will be hit with fruits such as kiwis, oranges, strawberries etc but can also be hit with vegetables such as bell peppers, broccoli, tomatoes, spinach etc. one thing to keep in mind is to avoid overcooking as vitamin c is heat sensitive and high temperatures will cause the vitamin c to oxidize.

so by implementing some choices from oats, brown rice, legumes (lentils/beans), beef liver, egg yolks, nutritional yeast, fortified cereals, fruits and vegetables you can hit your vitamin B and C. to easily check this you can log food into cronometer a food tracking app (im not affiliated) very easily to see if youre hitting the recommended daily intake for these at the very least.
