# can you target the different bicep heads?

Can you actually target the long and short head of the biceps separately? Should you be doing two different bicep exercises?

Its commonly told that you can target the different heads of the biceps by doing two different exercises like an incline curl and an preacher curl. is this true should you be doing two movements for the biceps?

contrary to what most people believe you cant target the different head of the biceps to any meaningful degree. why is this? when looking at the anatomy of the biceps we can see that their origin is different but they both insert at the same place on the elbow. this means no matter what elbow flexion exercise you do both heads of the biceps will be generating force.

so what should you do for the biceps? just pick a stable supinated curl variation. and if you want to bias it even more you can do something with a descending resistance profile like a preacher curl.
