# How do you know if your muscles are recovered?

How do you know if your muscles are recovered from working out?

we know that you want to be recovered from your training in order to keep progressing. but how can you actually know if you are recovered?

being recovered cant be measured through a physical feeling the only way to know you are recovered is if you are progressing week to week over time. something to keep in mind is that you can be recovered while being sore as you should base it on your performance.
