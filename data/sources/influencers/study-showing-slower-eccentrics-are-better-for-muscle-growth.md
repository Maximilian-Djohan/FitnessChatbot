# study showing slower eccentrics are better for muscle growth?

Study showing slower eccentrics are worse for muscle growth? Fast eccentrics buffed?

well first what is an eccentric? the eccentric of an exercise is the lowering phase when you are resisting the weight on the way down. this would be followed by the concentric where you would be pushing the weight. but is having a slower or faster eccentric better for muscle growth?

in a meta analysis on this topic it compares using a faster eccentric to a slower eccentric. the result of this was that it will be similar in terms of muscle growth but slightly favoring the faster eccentrics. so what should you do?
