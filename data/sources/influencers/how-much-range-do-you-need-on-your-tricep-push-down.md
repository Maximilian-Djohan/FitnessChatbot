# how much range do you need on your tricep push down?

How much range do you need on your tricep pushdown for the most muscle growth?

we know that your tricep performs elbow extension which is what is being trained in a pushdown but how much range should you be doing? well you will be working all parts of your tricep well regardless but the main purpose of your pushdown is for the longhead. so how much rom should you be using?

well we know that rom is arbitrary as you can grow in part of the range as seen in isometrics but how can you change your range to bias the longhead? some people start it at 90 degrees but in reality the longhead has the best leverage in a more flexed elbow meaning in those higher degrees in elbow extension. so what should you do?

well it will be preference. as long as youre starting around that 90-100+ degree range you will be good. so you should pick something that feels comfortable and helps you standardize your form. - also another factor to keep in mind is that more rom can help you decrease load which can lead to less stabilization demand on the shoulder.
