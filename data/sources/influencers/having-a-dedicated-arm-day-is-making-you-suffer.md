# having a dedicated arm day is making you suffer

Having an arm day is making your arms smaller

many people like to do a arm day to bring up their arms in comparison to the rest of their muscle groups. but doing this is actually counter intuitive but why is this?

if your running a 2 day or 3 day split then incorporate an arm day you will be extending how many days it takes you to train all your muscle groups. but this will in turn lower the frequency that you are training your arms which will be counter productive. so then what should you do to prioritize your arms?

Non-linear dose-response relationships of hypertrophy to workout volume vary substantially between meta-analyses, perhaps due to inclusion criteria.

its very simple no matter what split youre running if you want to prioritize your arms you just need to train them first. can it possibly interfere with your other muscle groups? yes. but thats a given with any change you do. if you want to prioritize your arms dont be afraid to prioritize them.
