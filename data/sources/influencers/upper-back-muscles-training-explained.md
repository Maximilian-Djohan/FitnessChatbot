# Upper Back Muscles Training Explained

Upper Back Training Explained

The upper back is made up of two main muscles the traps and rhomboids

The traps and rhomboids main function is scapular retraction (pulling shoulder blades back)

To train your upperback you want to perform the muscles main function

To train scapular retraction you should do a row with flared elbows

Keep in mind that your rear delts will be active during this as they are bringing your arms back (lats also help depending on arm angle)

You can limit other muscles involvement by doing a single joint movement like a Kelso shrug
