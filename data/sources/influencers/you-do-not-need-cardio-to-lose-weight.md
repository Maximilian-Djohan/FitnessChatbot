# You do NOT need cardio to lose weight

You do NOT need cardio to lose weight.

Many people say that you need to be active to lose weight, but is this true? While it does help to be active, you don't need to be. For some people weight loss will be more sustainable not performing cardio. So then why do people do cardio, and what should you be doing when you're losing weight?

Cardio exercises directly burn calories, helping to create a calorie deficit.

All you need to do is be in a calorie deficit. A majority of this should be happening through your diet. Yes, cardio can help with this as you are burning more calories, but for some people feeling obligated to do so can be unmotivating and in turn make them give up. So while it is beneficial, you can still do very well without it.
