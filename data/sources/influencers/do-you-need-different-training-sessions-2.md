# Do you need different training sessions?

Do you need different training sessions with varying exercises/order or should you keep them the same?

Upper A: Upper B:

people ask if they should keep exercise selection the same or change it by having different training days (e.g. FB a, FB b, FB c). is this a problem if you are still training all the same muscle groups? yes you will progress well doing this but with switching exercises there will be some fibers that wont get worked because of variation and wont benefit from a higher frequency even if the overall muscle group gets trained.

for exercise order it doesnt have the problem of missing muscle fibers but changing order can make it hard to track your progressive overload. if you were to curl at the start of your session one day and at the end of your session another day you will get less reps on that second day. its good to keep order the same so you can make sure you are progressing everything incase you need to make any changes. it can be fine if you want to change order but it wont be beneficial.

overall its best to keep variation to a minimum. you will be eliminating any variables and getting better at the movements you choose to do. but if you enjoy variation it is fine to do but it wont be beneficial / could be worse
