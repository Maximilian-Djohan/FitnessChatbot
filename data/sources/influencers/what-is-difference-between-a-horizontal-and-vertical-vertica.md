# what is difference between a horizontal and vertical shrug?

What is the difference between a vertical shrug and a horizontal/kelso shrug? Which is better for trap growth?

many people do a shrug to grow their traps. in a vertical shrug you are elevating the scapula bringing up and in a kelso shrug you are retracting the scapula bringing it back. but which is better in actually growing your traps?

well in reality the kelso shrug will better train the traps as they are better at scapula retraction. and in your vertical shrug where you are training scapula elevation you will be better training the levator scapulae which is a muscle that runs along the side of your neck.

so if your primary goal is to grow the traps you will be better training scapular retraction through a kelso shrug or upper back row. and if you want to better train your levator scapulae you can do a vertical shrug and if you hold importance in both then you can do both.
