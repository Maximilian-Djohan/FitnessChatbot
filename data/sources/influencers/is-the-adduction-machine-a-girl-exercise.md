# Is the adduction machine a girl exercise?

Is the adduction machine a girl exercise? Should you be training adductors?

some people associate this machine with being a "girl exercise". obviously there isn't a difference with exercises between gender but is training your adductors really important?

your adductors are very large and can contribute towards to around ~1/3 of your leg width. it's important to train them for your overall leg aesthetic and the only way to train all parts of your adductors is through training adduction. (hip extension won't train the entire adductor)
