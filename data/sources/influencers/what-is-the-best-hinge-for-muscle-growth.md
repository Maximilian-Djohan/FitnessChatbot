# What is the best hinge for muscle growth?

What is the best hinge for muscle growth? What is the difference between them.

there are many different hinge patterns to choose from but what is the difference between them? how do they affect what muscle is working? the difference between them besides stability will be where in the exercise it is the hardest. the 90 Degree Hyperextension will be hardest at the top, 45 Degree Hyperextension midway and SLDL at the bottom.

to see how the resistance will effect what muscles get worked depending on what you pick will come down to the muscle leverages for the muscles that perform hip extension. glutes you want the exercise to hardest at the top. hamstrings you want it to be hardest towards the middle and adductors towards the bottom. erectors will also contribute more towards the bottom.

Proportional contribution of the hip extensor muscles alters with joint angle?

so what does this mean? first keep in mind no matter what you choose depending on the rom all muscles will be contributing. but a 90 degree hyperextension will be the best for glutes, 45 degree hyperextension for the hamstrings and SLDL for the adductors/erectors.
