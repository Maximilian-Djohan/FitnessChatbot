# What is the best oblique exercise

What is the BEST oblique exercise?

to train the obliques you want to perform trunk rotation (twisting torso) and lateral flexion (bending torso sideways). you want to pick an exercise that is easy to progress and is stable for each of these functions.

to train trunk rotation you can perform this seated on a pecdec by rotating your torso with your arms on the weight.

to train lateral flexion you can do a sideways crunch type motion on a smith holding the bar with your opposite hand.

no oblique training won't make your waist blocky. it will make your midsection more aesthetic. if your midsection looks blocky that's just because you have a wide waist (brutal)
