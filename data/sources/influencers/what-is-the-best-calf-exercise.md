# what is the best calf exercise?

What is the BEST calf exercise?

to best train the calf you want to train the function of it and the main function of the calf is plantar flexion.

but another thing you want to keep in mind is to train the calf with a straight leg to avoid antagonistic inhibition as the calves are a biarticular muscle that crosses both the knee and the ankle. you also want to focus on only training in the bottom part of the rom as the gastroc has better leverage there.

so what exercise is good for this? any calf variation where you have a straight leg will be great.
