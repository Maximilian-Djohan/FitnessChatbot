# What is intermittent fasting? Is it an effective strategy for weight loss and gaining muscle?

What is intermittent fasting? Is it an effective strategy for weight loss and gaining muscle?

Well simply put intermittent fasting is where you restrict yourself from eating for a long period of time usually from night all the way till noon the next day essentially pushing back your first meal and possibly having your last meal earlier. This looks like having your first meal at noon and last meal at 8pm. This is essentially just restricting the hours of the day in which you can eat but is it actually effective?

Well it actually can be effective in certain scenarios. If you arent doing anything intense in the mornings or wake up late and dont require energy it can be used. This can help if you often find yourself hungrier over multiple smaller meals as instead you can funnel calories in 2-3 meals and help reduce snacking and as a side effect help you easier stay in a caloric deficit. But what about muscle growth?

Some people might worry that restricting your meal times can hinder muscle growth as it also relies on your nutrition. But the most important factor is hitting your protein, and your macros. After this then yes splitting your protein between 2-3 meals can help to elevate muscle protein synthesis periodically throughout your eating time frame.

So is it effective? Yes it can be dependent on the person. Its not inherently better than eating another way but these type of dieting strategies can be use in certain scenarios if you know how to structure your eating correctly. If they help you stick to and work towards your goals then go for it.
