# What is a schizo warmup?

What is schizo warmup? Should you be "schizomaxxing" to increase muscle growth?

In the context of exercise the "schizo rep" is a warmup method. It is a method done by doing a "fake" super fast rep without resistance.

What is a "schizo rep"? The "schizo rep" is a fast rep done before your working set to induce the post activation potentiation effect. PAP is the increase of performance after a contraction. So you want to induce PAP before your working set to increase your performance on it.

Post-activation potentiation (PAP) refers to the temporary increase in muscle force production following a conditioning contraction or exercise.

How should the "schizo rep" be performed? It should be done after all your warmup sets with adequate rest between them. Then do the rep and go straight into your working set. You want to do the rep as fast as possible. When you are going as fast as you can you will be recruiting the most muscle fibers as you will have maximal effort. And it's important to go right into your set because PAP will be peaked for 10-15 seconds.

Voluntarily increasing central motor command increases perception of effort, and the number of recruited motor units (and the number of activated muscle fibers).

So basically do a normal warmup then a schizo rep into your working set. Or if you don't want to do a schizo rep then don't, but you should still do a ~90% one rep warmup in your normal warmup to induce PAP.
