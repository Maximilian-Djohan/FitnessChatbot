# Are jeff nippards back tips good for back growth?

Are Jeff Nippard's back exercise tips effective for muscle growth?

the first tip is to not round your back forward on pulling movements. this is a great tip. many people think when they round forward they will get more rom on their lats but they are actually extending their spine forward. then as they pull they extend their spine back. this wont be training the lats and will instead be training the erectors which will take away from the lats.

his next tip is to not go all the way up on pulldowns. this makes sense and is also applicable to pulldowns (dont go all the way down) as when your arms are fully adducted to your side other muscles such as your chest will contribute to the movement.

so yes these are both effective tips you can incorporate into your training for better form.
