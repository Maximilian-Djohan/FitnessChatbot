# How to make any exercise harder?

How to make any machine harder and have a customizable resistance profile

some people might want to change the resistance profile of their machine or make it heavier without the purchase of a gym pin. so how can you do this with minimal equipment? well first you need to understand what a machine cam is. a cam is the rotating circular disc where the belt/cable runs along on your machine during your rep. altering this will make these changes possible.

well how does the cable/cam determine the resistance profile of the exercise? the cam will rotate around the pivot during your rep. wherever the pivot is farthest away from the cable/line of force due to the cam length it will be the hardest and wherever its the closest it will be the easiest. so if its hard at the start its due to the longest part being at the start to make it descending. so how can we change this?

well using something like your cuffs you can place it on a certain part of the cam to make the cable run farther away from the pivot which will make it harder overall and especially harder during that part of the ROM. this is easily done at the start where you can just wedge it in making it descending.

imagine the red are your cuffs where you put at the start. this will make the cable rotate further away from the pivot making it harder overall and descending since its at the start of the movement
