to understand the purpose of this you have to understand Sherrington's Law of Irradiation. the law of irradiation explains how when you contract a nearby muscle it can increase the performance and recruit more muscle fibers. so what does this mean?

Sherrington's Law of Irradiation, also known as the irradiation principle, describes how a contracting muscle can induce a neurological overflow to surrounding muscles, increasing their activation and potentially enhancing strength and stability.

when clenching a bluetooth handle you will contract nearby muscles and in turn better recruit surrounding muscles. examples of where this can be used is on a lateral raise or tricep extension where you cuff above the wrist. so might as well clench something if nothing is in your hand to increase performance on your set.
