# How to Limit Bicep Involvement in your rows @bigprotalks

How to limit bicep involvement in your rows

to limit bicep involvement in your rows your seat/grip are very important. what you are looking for is to reduce the torque at the elbow during the pull. to do this on a close grip row you want to use a higher seat and on a wide grip you want to use a wider grip. what you are looking for is to have a stacked/aligned wrist/elbow as you pull towards the end of the movement.
