# Why do some people band exercises?

Why do some people band their exercises? Should you band your exercises for more muscle growth?

sometimes you can see people adding a band on their exercises but what purpose does this serve? are they just doing it to ego lift? no. they do this to alter the resistance profile of the exercise to match with the joint action they are training.

Resistance profile curve types (Force vs Joint angle): Ascending, Bell-shaped, Descending

in the example of a JM Press we would use a band to make it easier towards the bottom of the lift as it takes away resistance there. this is where more shoulder flexion/adduction can occur which would put more load on the chest/front delt. but with it banded it will be hardest where the medial/lateral head of the tricep will be contributing the most.

in the example of a squat pattern there will be both hip extension for the glutes/adductor magnus and knee extension for the quads. as you get deeper into the squat pattern there will be a greater degree of hip extension occurring relative to how much knee extension is happening meaning it will train the glutes/adductor magnus better especially in that range. but banding it will make it easier at that range and making it harder.
