# bench if you want to increase your bench

Should you bench if you want to increase your bench? or should you do more "optimal" exercises?

with the popularization of science based lifting more people are ditching compound exercises like the bench for single joint movements like a chest fly as it will be better for growing your chest. but is this something you should switch to if you want to increase your bench?

no. you should still continue to bench as you will develop/maintain neural/coordination adaptations from performing your bench press. so if you want to increase your bench you should keep doing it. but other factors like frequency/rep ranges that science based lifters can be applied to help increase your bench.
