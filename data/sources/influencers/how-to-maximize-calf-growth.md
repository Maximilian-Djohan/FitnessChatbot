# how to maximize calf growth

how to MAXIMIZE calf muscle growth

the calf is made up of the medial/lateral head of the gastrocnemius and the soleus. to maximize calf growth we want to have an exercise that best trains each part of the calf

for the gastrocnemius the main part of the calf you will do a bottom rom calf raise. but you will do two sets. one set will have your feet internally rotated to better train the lateral head and one set externally rotated which will better train the medial head.

then for the soleus you want to do a seated calf raise as it will disadvantage the gastrocnemius

so in total you can do these three different exercises. but for a majority of people it will be fine to just do a normal bottom rom calf raise as it will effectively train all parts of the calf
