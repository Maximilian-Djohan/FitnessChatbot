# How to fix pain on the TNF Press

How to help fix wrist/shoulder pain on the TNF incline press

some people say they feel wrist pain or say its uncomfortable on their shoulders so how can we fix this? well the first thing we can change is the usage of safety stoppers. many people who will have wrist pain will be due to unracking the weight having their wrist in a compromised position. instead using the stoppers you can start it already unracked to avoid this. you can also have it higher to avoid going deeper into the rom which can be uncomfortable on your shoulders.

the next fix is your arm path and how you execute it. you want to be pressing with the bar landing around the bottom of your chest and you need to make sure your wrist and elbows are stacked directly on top of each other at the bottom of the exercise this will help alleviate some strain on the wrist. you also shouldnt be worrying about locking at the top as it will be very uncomfortable.

so takeaways from this should be having a good armpath, stacked joints and using the smith stoppers. if this still feels uncomfortable its fine theres no special reason to pick this its just another option. you can just choose another movement which also challenges shoulder flexion for the upper pec.
