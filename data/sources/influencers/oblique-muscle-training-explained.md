# Oblique Muscle Training Explained

Oblique Training Explained

similar to abs, people overcomplicate their training by doing circuits or high volume through things like twists / crunches.

Your obliques are made up of two muscles, the internal obliques and external obliques.

External oblique

To train your obliques you need to train its main functions.

the main functions of your obliques is trunk rotation (twisting torso) and lateral flexion (bending torso sideways).

(also help with abdominal compression and spinal flexion)

rotation: flexion:

to train trunk rotation you can do a cable rotation / machine (read desc for reasoning)

to train lateral flexion you can do a sideways crunch on a hyperextension
