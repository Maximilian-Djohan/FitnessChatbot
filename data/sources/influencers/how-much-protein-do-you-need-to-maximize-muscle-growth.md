# how much protein do you need to maximize muscle growth?

How much protein should you be consuming to maximize muscle growth?

protein is important in building muscle through muscle protein synthesis. its also commonly stated that the more protein you eat the better but is this actually true and what is the recommended amount of protein to maximize muscle growth?

Muscle protein synthesis (MPS) is the metabolic process where the body incorporates amino acids into new muscle proteins.

To maximize growth when resistance training all you need is 0.7-0.8 grams of protein per pound of body weight. this is all that is needed and trying to eat more than this wont increase "gains".
