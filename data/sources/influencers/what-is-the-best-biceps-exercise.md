# What is the best biceps exercise?

What is the BEST biceps EXERCISE?

to train the biceps we want to bias it in comparison to the other elbow flexors. we do this with wrist position and resistance profiles. for the biceps it gets advantaged with a descending resistance profile (hardest at bottom) and a supinated grip. what is a good exercise for this?

Elbow flexor moment arm lengths increase with increasing elbow flexion and are maximal in the contracted position. The brachioradialis has very poor leverage when lengthened and the best leverage when shortened.

Biceps brachii moment arm lengths are longer when the wrist is supinated (although only in the middle of the elbow joint angle range of motion). This means that we can therefore target the biceps brachii with supinated grip biceps curls, and the brachioradialis with pronated grip biceps curls.

the perfect exercise you can do for this is a preacher curl with a ~60 degree bench angle. doing this we are making it descending with the hardest part around where the bicep has best leverage while being supinated to advantage it more.

(you can also do any machine/normal bench variation)
