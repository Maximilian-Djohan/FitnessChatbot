# What is the quinton press

What is the Quinton Press? Is it an effective tricep exercise?

The Quinton Press is an alternative tricep exercise to an overhead extension/JM press for the medial/lateral head. This will happen with the shoulder angle changing throughout the movement, the medial/lateral head will be the main parts contributing over the long head. And this might look weird as the resistance is on the elbow, but you can think of it as doing a JM press against the wall.

And some people might worry about upper pec/front delt being active as it looks like there is shoulder flexion occurring, but this won't be the case. As you perform the exercise, shoulder flexion won't be challenged/loaded well.

So is this a good exercise? Yes, it's a good alternative to other medial/lateral head exercises with its stability and easy setup. It also matches the strength profile of the medial/lateral head being hardest towards lockout, unlike a JM press.
