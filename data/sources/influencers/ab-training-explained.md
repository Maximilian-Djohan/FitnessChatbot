# Ab Training Explained

The most overcomplicated muscle.

People tend to overcomplicate their ab training by doing some ab circuit, high volume, and excessive amount of exercises.

To know how to effectively train the abs let's first understand what they are.

Your abs are split into two regions, the Rectus Abdominis Upper and Rectus Abdominis Lower.

The biggest misconception people make is that they need to do multiple exercises to hit each region or each "angle" of their ab which is false.

To train your Abs you need to perform its main function being trunk flexion (bringing your upper torso closer to your hips).

You only need one exercise to perform this. You should do something that is stable and easily progressable such as a crunch variation.

Lower ab training is propaganda, the only reason why they wouldn't show is due to body fat percentage.

And yes while diet is more important to show your abs, training them will increase their size.
