# is upper lower 6x a good split?

Is Upper Lower 6x a week actually a good split?

Some people that want to get 3x weekly frequency without running full body or people that like to work out everyday ask whether running upper lower 6x is a good option. So what are factors that would make it a good or bad split?

The benefits of running upper lower 6x is to get 3x frequency instead of 2/2.3x frequency at the cost of your rest days. This will mean that muscle damage from previous days will cause CNS fatigue that will affect your next training days. Even if you arent training the same muscle consecutive days this CNS fatigue will affect the entire body. So what does this mean?

High volume squat workouts cause central nervous system (CNS) fatigue that takes more than 72 hours to recover from.

It can be effective to run upper lower 6x as long as you are making sure to progress and not overwhelm yourself. This can be using lower volume than you normally do (still hitting all the muscle regions you want to train), making sure to utilize a lower rep range and have all external factors in check.
