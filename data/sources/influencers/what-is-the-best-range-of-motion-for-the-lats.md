Why should you only train in a certain range? Your targeted muscle will have better force production in these ranges and will not have to compete with other muscles (ex. pecs/triceps above 120 degrees on pulldown)

The latissimus dorsi has three main regions (thoracic, lumbar, and pelvic) and they each have very different leverages for shoulder extension and adduction.

Shoulder extension moment arms peak between 25-55 degrees

The lats have minimal leverage for shoulder extension when the arms are overhead (the Inferior region has most)

Shoulder adduction moment arms peak between 60-75 degrees

The inferior (pelvic) and middle (lumbar) regions are the best shoulder adductors

Based on: Ackland, D.C., Pak, P., Richardson, M., & Pandy, M.G. (2008). Moment arms of the muscles crossing the anatomical shoulder. Journal of Anatomy, 213(4), 383-390.
