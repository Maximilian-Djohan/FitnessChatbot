# how to fix your sleep schedule?

How to fix your sleep schedule?

people often find themselves messing up their sleep schedule which can end up turning into a bad habit of staying up late or just lead to sleep deprivation/less time sleeping which will be detrimental to your health. so how can we fix this? well its simple your sleep schedule is determined by your "internal clock" (circadian rhythm). so how can we "fix" it to get you back to normal?

CIRCADIAN RHYTHM

well first we need to determine what affects your circadian rhythm first and most importantly we can start with your sleep/wake hygiene. ideally the goal is to sleep and wake at the same time everyday. your body relaxation/arousal is heavily impacted by light so at night dimming lights 2 hours prior to bed/avoiding bright screens and sleeping in a "blacked out room". next will be your wake which can be getting direct sunlight or very bright light within 30-60 minutes of waking up (10-20minutes).

CIRCADIAN RHYTHM

next will be more external controls that you should keep in mind or consider adding. avoiding caffeine completely on the day you want to fix your sleep, avoiding naps, usage of melatonin 3 hours prior to bed to "artificially" start the sleep cycle earlier (15mg), and the usage of other supplements can be used for relaxation (magnesium glycinate, glycine etc).

its quite easy to fix your sleep via hygiene and usage of supplements but you have to be intentional and incorporate all of the advice as a whole. a lot of people will do something like stay awake for the next day or wake up early in order to fix it but end up doom scrolling, bed rotting and spamming caffeine which defeats the purpose of actually trying to fix your sleep. you can drastically improve or fix it in 1 day if not 2 days. every day after that will come naturally in comparison and it will be a breeze if you continue all of this after youve fixed it as well.
