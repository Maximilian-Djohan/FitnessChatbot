Common chest exercises and their more "optimal" alternatives

The most common chest exercises are freeweight presses. but how can you make your chest training more optimal?

well the next step you could take is eliminating any stability demand. as you eliminate the stability demand you will have a lower perception of effort which allow for more motor unit recruitment and in turn more muscle fibers trained

then the next/last important change you can make is making the exercise single joint. by doing this you can make sure to only train the specific joint action being adduction and flexion as well as eliminating any elbow extension.

so what exercises should you include in your chest workout? any of these options will work. but obviously as you pick more "optimal" they will be better for muscle growth which will be more prominent over a longer period of time
