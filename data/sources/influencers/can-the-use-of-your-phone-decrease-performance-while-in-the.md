# can the use of your phone decrease performance while in the gym?

Are phones vaulted? Using your phone in the gym can decrease muscle growth?

many people are on their phones in between sets as they rest. but a study shows that performance goes down after being on your phone during a rest period. they compared a group who rested while on social media on their phone to a group watching documentaries. the group who was on social media had a higher decrease in reps afterward. but why is this?

Exerting mental effort by using social media on a smartphone reduces the capacity to create a central motor command (and hence produce motor unit recruitment).

Research

when youre on your scrolling through your phone it will cause "mental fatigue". this will lower your motor unit recruitment as it will impair your central motor command. and in turn this will decrease your performance as less muscle fibers will be activated and result in lower reps. so what should you do?

Voluntarily increasing central motor command increases perception of effort, and the number of recruited motor units (and the number of activated muscle fibers)
