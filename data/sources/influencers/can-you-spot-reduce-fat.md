# Can you spot reduce fat?

Can you spot reduce fat?

many people wanting to lose fat want to "spot reduce" it. they want to target a specific area of their body through exercise such as their stomach. but is it actually possible to reduce fat in a specific area through exercise?

Spot reduction, the idea that specific exercises can reduce fat in targeted

it is a myth that you can spot reduce fat. the only way to lose fat is through a calorie deficit where you are burning more calories than you eat through diet and the assistance of exercise. you will lose fat throughout your body at different rates dependent on genetics. but if you continue to be in a deficit you will eventually lose fat in the area that you want.

What is a calorie deficit? A calorie deficit occurs when you burn more calories than you consume
