# What muscles are worked in a dip?

What muscles are worked in a dip? Does the chest get any good muscle growth from it?

we know to train a muscle we want to train its functions, so what functions get trained in a dip? it will be mainly elbow extension, shoulder flexion and a bit of horizontal adduction. we know that elbow extension will be training the tricep but will the chest get any good work?

going over the functions being trained starting with shoulder flexion (bringing your arms from behind to in front of you) some people might think your upper chest will be worked in this as you are performing shoulder flexion but it will actually be front delt as it has best leverage in this range of shoulder flexion. and in terms of horizontal adduction for mid/lower pec that will depend on your grip width. the farther out you go the more chest work you will get.

so overall it will mainly be a medial/lateral head triceps exercise with some front delt, and there will be some chest stimulus depending how wide you go but realistically it won't be anything significant and you shouldn't rely on it to count towards your chest vol
