# how to make your back extension actually train your back

How to make your back extension train your back instead of your legs

the back extension is often thought as an lower back aka erector exercise but this isnt the case. your erectors will be playing a stabilizing role as you arent actively bending the spine rather just performing hip extension which makes it a good hamstring exercise. so how should you perform it to make it a back exercise?

well it simple if you want to make it more erectors you want to isolate the action of spinal extension. you can do this by raising the pad up so you wont be bending at the hips and rather be only bending at the spine.

but keep in mind for most people training your erectors directly wont be needed and training it through its stabilizing role during your hip hinge will be enough.
