# Prioritizing the upper chest is overrated

Prioritizing the upper chest is overrated. You do NOT need to prioritize your upper chest.

people promote prioritizing your upper chest over your mid/lower pec for an "aesthetic" look but this shouldnt be the case. when looking at your chest anatomy your mid/lower pec takes up more area than your upper pec. your upper pec contributes a lot less to your chest visual and in turn you should be prioritizing your mid/lower pec.
