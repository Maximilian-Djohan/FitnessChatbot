# What is a recline curl? - picture is from @scivinhtist

What is a recline curl, are they better than preacher curls?

A recline curl is very similar to a preacher curl except for the fact that they don't have a flexed shoulder. This means that your arm is to the side of your body instead of in front of you. But what does this mean?

The idea behind this is that as your shoulder isn't flexed or even extended, it will have better leverage. In data this is shown as a preacher curl will have INSIGNIFICANTLY "more" brachialis than biceps growth as it has a flexed shoulder. So what should you do?

The difference between a flexed and nonflexed shoulder is insignificant, with the study having flaws, but in theory if you want to "maximize" bicep growth you'd want to keep a neutral shoulder like in a recline curl. This is also under the assumption that you can maintain your stability and comfortability doing this.
