# Sample Anterior Day

Sample Anterior Day

Exercises & Alternatives

To effectively structure an anterior day we want to perform all the joint actions of the muscles and muscle regions we want to train. so what would this look like?

MUSCLE GROUP PRIMARY JOINT ACTIONS
Chest Horizontal adduction
Shoulder flexion

Front/Side Delt Shoulder flexion
Shoulder abduction

Triceps Elbow extension

Quads Knee extension

for the chest starting off with your main movement training horizontal adduction (bringing upper arm to midline) can be either any flat press or pec dec/fly. then a secondary movement training shoulder flexion will be an incline press or a low to high fly.

for the shoulder you want to train shoulder abduction (bringing upper arm up to the side). for the front delts this can be through a shoulder press machine/variant. and for the side delt you can do any lateral raise variation I'd recommend machine or cable.

lastly for the triceps you just need to extend the elbow. you can do this through any pushdown variation. and if you want to add another exercise alongside you can do something like a JM press or dip.

for the quads you want to isolate knee extension. this will be done through a leg extension. (no squat pattern since it will overlap with the posterior day)

so train all the muscles you care about by picking exercises that perform their function. and for volume use what you progress fastest from. I'd recommend starting at 2 sets per muscle group split between two exercise and experimenting from there by adding sets. and of course perform everything to 0 rir/failure ideally around something like a 5-9 rep range.
