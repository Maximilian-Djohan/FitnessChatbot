# under eating can lower your tdee

Under eating can actually slow your weight loss?

many people in the pursuit of weightloss start to heavily under eat or even starve themselves to do so. we know to lose weight you want to be in an calorie deficit. so if you heavily under eat wouldnt you be in a calorie deficit?

What is a calorie deficit? A calorie deficit occurs when you burn more calories than you consume

it would but while you might think "starving" will speed up the process it might not. why is this? your maintenance calories is the energy used by your body in a day. this is made up of three parts BMR, thermogenesis and physical activity. in the thermogenesis it counts the energy your body used to process the food you eat. so while not eating can increase your deficit it could also lower your maintenance and counteract that.

so what does this mean? you should still be eating a healthy amount of food and avoid starving yourself. dont try to rush the process.
