Does training matter when you're on gear? Should you listen to enhanced people for advice?

We know that when you're on gear/PEDs you will obviously grow a lot faster than a natural. Many people look up to people on PEDs as they have impressive physiques. But how much does their training actually matter and should you listen to their training advice?

In bodybuilding, "gear" typically refers to anabolic steroids or other performance-enhancing drugs used to accelerate muscle growth and strength gains.

In a study comparing naturals who didn't train, naturals that trained, enhanced no training and enhanced training the results might surprise you. So how much does their training really matter?

Another study tested people in groups with varying levels of testosterone. 5 groups had different levels of test at 25, 50, 125, 300, 600 mg of test. This shows that when you're enhanced your growth can be considered independent from your training as it will be an external stimulus from your training.
