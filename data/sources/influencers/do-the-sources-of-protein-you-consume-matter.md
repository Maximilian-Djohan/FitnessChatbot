# do the sources of protein you consume matter?

Do protein sources actually matter? what protein sources should you count towards your total?

some people say you should count protein from things like grains or vegetables as they are incomplete and only count protein from animal sources like meat and dairy products. but is this true and how should you track your protein?

well what actually does "incomplete" mean? your body has 9 essential amino acids and some plant sources will be lacking in a few of those causing it to be "incomplete" unlike animal products. this might sound bad but this isnt the case. while one source might be lacking you can still cover all essential amino acids by adding in another source that covers what it lacks.

this sounds good but some people still might not trust this. so what does this look like when comparing outcomes when building muscle? this is reinforced when looking at data as there is no significant differences between the plant vs non plant groups. so what should you take away from this?
