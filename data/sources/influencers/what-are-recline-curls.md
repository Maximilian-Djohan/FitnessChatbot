# What are recline curls?

What is a recline curl are they better sl in preacher curls?

A recline curl is very similar to a preacher curl except for the fact that they dont have a flexed shoulder. this means that your arm is to the side of your body instead of infront of you. but what does this mean?

the idea behind this is that as your shoulder isnt flexed or even extended that it will have better leverage. in data this is shown as a preacher curl will have INSIGNIFICANTLY "more" brachialis than biceps growth as it has a flexed shoulder. so what should you do?

the difference between a flexed and nonflexed shoulder is insignificant with the study having flaws but in theory if you want to "maximize" bicep growth youd want to keep a neutral shoulder like in a recline curl. this is also under the assumption that you can maintain your stability and comfortability doing this.
