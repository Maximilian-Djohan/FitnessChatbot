# What is mechanical Tension?

What is mechanical tension and how does it cause muscle growth/hypertrophy?

the basic explanation for mechanical tension is that when performing an exercise there will be tension. this tension will create pulling forces on the muscle which will cause muscle growth but how?

Mechanical tension, in the context of strength training and muscle growth, refers to the force muscles experience when they are stretched or contracted against resistance.

this happens through mechanotransduction. in your muscles you will have mechanosensors which will detect this tension and in turn create signals for growth.

Mechanotransduction is the process by which cells convert mechanical stimuli into biochemical signals, leading to changes in cell behavior and function. This process is crucial for various biological functions, including development, tissue repair, and sensory perception.
