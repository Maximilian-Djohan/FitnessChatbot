# What is the best glute exercise?

What is the BEST glute exercise?

to train the glutes we want to train its function of hip extension where the glutes has best leverage compare to other muscles that also extend the hip. the glutes has best leverage at a short length which is around lockout. what is a good exercise for this?

a great exercise for this is a 90 degree hip extension where you are focusing on the top/end rom as it will be hardest at lockout compared to something like a 45 extension.
