# Common quad exercises and their more optimal alternative

Common quad exercises and their more 'optimal' alternative

the most common quad exercises are freeweight squats. but how can you make your quad training more optimal?

well the next step you could take is eliminating any stability demand. as you eliminate the stability demand you will have a lower perception of effort which will allow for more motor unit recruitment and in turn more muscle fibers trained.

then the biggest change you can do is turning it from a compound movement where you have your adductors/glutes active as there will be hip extension to only isolating the quads role in knee extension which can be done in a leg extension.
