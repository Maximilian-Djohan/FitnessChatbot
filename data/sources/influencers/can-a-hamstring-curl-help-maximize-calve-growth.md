# can a hamstring curl help maximize calve growth?

can a hamstring curl potentially help maximize calve growth?

it might sound weird that you need a hamstring exercise to help grow your calves but what is the reasoning behind this? the gastrocnemius of the calf crosses both the knee and ankle joint and in turn has some contribution in knee flexion. this is also shown as the calf shown to have muscle activation in a hamstring curl.

now obviously you shouldnt think about doing a hamstring curl to train your calves as they wont be contributing much to the movement. but say if your ankle is injured making sure you have a hamstring curl can help if youre worried about atrophy in the gastrocnemius.
