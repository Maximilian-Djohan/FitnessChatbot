# Why hasn't jacks fit progressed

Why hasn't Jacksfit Progressed?

Jacksfit is a popular creator who is known for their "bodybuilding" journey he has started off very skinny and despite some progress stayed that way. but why is this? what is holding him back?

the first conclusion people would come to is that he isn't eating enough. this is true but this isn't the reason. if he was actually consistent, trained with intensity, tried to progress and had better exercise selection/execution he would progress despite his subpar diet.

this is also something that is overexaggerated in importance your training should be the most important thing and yes eating matters but no amount of optimized eating will make up for sub par or lack of stimulus.
