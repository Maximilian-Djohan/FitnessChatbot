# What are the best hamstring exercises?

what are the BEST hamstring exercises for muscle growth?

to maximize hamstring growth you want to train its functions. to do that you want to train both knee flexion and hip extension. you want to pick a variation which is stable and easily progressable so which exercises are the best for this?

to train knee flexion it is simple just pick a hamstring curl variation. either will train the entire hamstring efficiently.

but for hip extension as there are other muscles contributing to that joint action you want to make sure you are training the hamstring where it has best leverage. this can be done in a 45 degree hyperextension where it is hardest in the mid range.

so do both hamstring curl and a hinge to train the entire hamstring. and yes while you can do just a hamstring curl to train the entire hamstring doing a hinge will provide more proximal growth closer to the hip.
