# does your lat pulldown train the upper back?

Does a lat pulldown train your upper back muscles?

we know that a lat pulldown will train the lats but does it also train your upper back? well what is occurring during the movement? It will be primarily be shoulder adduction which will be training the lats. but it can also have some scapular depression and retraction during it. so does this mean it trains the upper back as well?

while your upper back will have some activation during the movement as you can be moving the scapula a bit the primary thing happening will be shoulder adduction throughout the rep. there wont be any significant stimulus with the slight scapula movement so you shouldnt count it towards your upper back training.
