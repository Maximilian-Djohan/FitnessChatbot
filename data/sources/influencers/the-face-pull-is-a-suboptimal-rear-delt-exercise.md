# the face pull is a suboptimal rear delt exercise

The face pull is NOT a good rear delt exercise

one of the most popular rear delt exercises is the face pull. it trains the rear delts function of horizontal abduction by bringing the arms backwards. but if it trains this function then isnt it a good rear delt exercise?

well as your arm bends as its coming back it causes people to retract their scapula using their traps in the exercise. so with this arm path it essentially becomes an unstable upper back row as you have no support. so if you want to isolate your rear delts what should you do?

the best way to isolate your rear delts function of horizontal abduction is through a reverse pec dec. so do this if you want to isolate them but keep in mind if you are doing any pull where your arm is infront of you or any upper back row your rear delts will get worked very well already
