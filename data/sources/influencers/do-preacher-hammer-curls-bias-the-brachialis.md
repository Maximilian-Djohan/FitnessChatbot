# Do preacher hammer curls bias the brachialis?

Preacher Hammer Curls are VAULTED. they do NOT BIAS the brachialis

some creators claim that you can bias the brachialis through something like a preacher hammer curl but is this actually true? a preacher hammer curl whether its descending or ascending will NOT BIAS the brachialis. but why is this? (common machines like Planet Fitness and Hammer STR are bell curved)

what determines what gets "biased" in an elbow flexion? it will be decided through what has best leverage throughout the ROM in which you match the resistance profile along with it. so why doesnt a hammer preacher curl target it? regardless of the resistance profile of the preacher curl throughout a majority of the rom either your biceps or brachioradialis will have best leverage

Elbow flexor moment arm lengths increase with increasing elbow flexion and are maximal in the contracted position. The brachioradialis has very poor leverage when lengthened and the best leverage when shortened.

so how can you bias the brachialis? youd want to do a descending reverse curl staying in only the first 20 degrees of rom. why is this? your brachialis will only have better leverage compared to the other elbow flexors in those first 20 degrees. and you choose to pronate your wrists as it will slightly disadvantage the biceps.

Biceps brachii moment arm lengths are longer when the wrist is supinated (although only in the middle of the elbow joint angle range of motion). This means that we can therefore target the biceps brachii with supinated grip biceps curls, and the brachioradialis with pronated grip biceps curls.

SUMMARY

regardless 99% of people shouldnt be trying to bias their brachialis as you will be training it very well in your descending bicep curl movement.
