# how much does enjoyment matter in exercise selection?

How much does exercise enjoyment actually matter for muscle growth?

there are many exercises which are considered "optimal" in which people say you should replace more traditional exercises with them. but many people dont want to make the switch because it isnt as enjoyable for them. but does enjoyment impact muscle growth?

enjoyment is important in training as it will correlate with motivation. if you are doing an exercise you dont like you will have less motivation to do so. with less motivation you will have an higher perception of effort which will decrease motor unit recruitment. this will in turn lead to less muscle growth.

Motor unit recruitment is a measure of how many motor neurons are activated in a particular muscle

but some exercises are objectively better. enjoyment wont make up for the benefits of leverages, resistance profiles or muscles targeted in an exercise. there should be a middle ground. at the least you should try to pick a stable exercise that fits the strength profile of the muscle you are trying to train. but if you enjoy a slightly worse variation its fine to do that instead.

so enjoyment AND optimization go hand in hand. dont force yourself to do something if you truly are against it. here is a balance something like a pulldown vs a fly. there will be a difference but you will still be training the muscle effectively regardless of what you pick
