# Is stretch mediated hypertrophy vaulted?

Is stretch mediated hypertrophy vaulted? Does SMH actually cause more muscle growth?

most people just assume stretch mediated hypertrophy is additional stimulus gained from doing an exercise where there is a stretch? most people assume SMH is just extra growth that is always available when doing a stretch positioned exercise. but what actually is SMH?

SMH is muscle growth due to a process known as sarcomerogenesis. This increases passive tension in the muscle promoting growth until you max out these SMH adaptations (cant benefit from sarcomerogenesis) and wont benefit from it. so doesnt this mean SMH is important?

Sarcomerogenesis is the process of adding sarcomeres in series within a muscle fiber, essentially lengthening the muscle by increasing the number of functional units.

SMH is "temporary" while you can benefit from it and maintain it you can lose it within a few weeks of not maintaining it. SMH is easy to gain and lose and has been overblown for its benefit. but why not maintain it if its not harmful?

During eccentric training, fascicle lengths increase rapidly for the first two months and then plateau in the third month (while pennation angles do not change).
