Should you train fasted?

some people say that you should train fasted to
burn more weight as you will be running off your
fat stores but is this true? is there any benefit
to training fasted?

Fasted Weight Training ¢

Fasted weight training refers to performing weightlifting exercises without
consuming any food or beverages prior to or during the workout. This

in Germs of weight loss it wont matter whether you are
training fasted or not. whether you burn your "fat
stores" then eat later or burn what you eat and still
have your "fat stores" it wont matter. the main thing is
your calorie deficit which will be the same regardless.

What is a calorie deficit? A calorie deficit occurs when you burn more
calories than you consume

so training fasted wont net you any benefits

but you will have the downside of not having

carbohydrates before your workout which will
lead you to have worse energy in the gym

In weight training, carbs provide the energy needed to fuel intense
workouts by supplying glucose, which is stored as glycogen in muscles.
