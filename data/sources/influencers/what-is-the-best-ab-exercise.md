# What is the best ab exercise?

What is the BEST ab exercise?

to train the abs we want to perform trunk flexion. we want to pick an exercise that is easy to progress and is stable. (keep in mind you can't bias lower vs upper abs as everything is connected so when you perform an exercise the entire muscle will contract).

any crunch variation will be good for this. (you aren't moving at the hips you are bending the spine)
