# how often should you switch your training?

How often should you switch up your training?

some people like to switch up their training periodically but what should you do? there are two things you could change in your training. the first being through exercise selection and the second being exercise order. but how often should you be changing up your training?

You should typically switch up your training every 4-6 weeks to prevent plateaus

for exercise selection the only time you should be changing your exercises is when you are going from a less "optimal" movement to a better one. i.e chest pressing -> chest flys. yes you can also change things up for enjoyment but it wont necessarily be beneficial and you also have to make sure the exercise is very similar to what you did prior.

for exercise order this is what you should be changing. but this is something you will only need to change after long periods of time. this can be sorted in "blocks" of time where you are bringing up a weakpoint in your physique structuring your training around it until you reach that goal then you can make a new one.
