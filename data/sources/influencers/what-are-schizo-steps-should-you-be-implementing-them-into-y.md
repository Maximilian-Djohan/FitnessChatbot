# What are schizo steps? should you be implementing them into your day to day life

What are schizo steps? should you be implementing them into your day to day life?

i dont feel like i need to explain them but some people have asked me to but its as simple as just walking throughout your day when you otherwise might be sitting. examples of this can be walking while eating your food, while youre waiting for something or watching a show at home, or if youre on your phone.

but what is the benefit of said steps? well its just to increase your energy expenditure. in general a higher amount of steps around 8k+ is shown to be correlated with lowering the risk of disease and mortality. but in a bodybuilding context it can just help you get more activity in to allow you to eat more or be in a deficit easier as you dont have to cut as many calories from food.

Daily steps and all-cause mortality: a meta-analysis of 15 international cohorts

PMCID: PMC9289978 NIHMSID: NIHMS1787838 PMID: 35247352

so yes if you find yourself just sitting down you can get some activity in if you want. it will only serve to benefit you as walking is very light and wont impact your resistance training. one thing i would personally avoid is walking in between your sets as it can take you longer for your heart rate to settle back down and in turn make you take longer to feel ready for your next set.
