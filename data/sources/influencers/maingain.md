# maingain

how WE will reverse the damage of sam sulek's mandated bulk to 200 pounds

sam sulek a popular lifting creator/body builder in the past spread the idea that you should bulk to 200lbs to not be "victim weight" this has caused many beginner lifters in the first few years of training to excessively bulk and put on unnecessary fat.

Excessive bulk refers to too much fat gain during a fitness bulk

this weight gain is just cope. being in such an excessive surplus does not benefit you. your body will only be putting on grams of muscle in a day which only takes 100-200 calories which occurs in your maintenance calories. so how are we going to fix this?
