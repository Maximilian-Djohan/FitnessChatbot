# What is the best preacher curl angle?

What is the best preacher curl bench angle?

When you are determining the angle of your preacher curl you want it to be descending - harder at the bottom where your biceps have better leverage over the other elbow flexors. but if you want to make it even better you want it to be hardest exactly where they have best leverage avoiding being the hardest in that 0-20 range as the brachialis has better leverage there. so what incline does this?

Elbow flexor moment arm lengths increase with increasing elbow flexion and are maximal in the contracted position. The brachioradialis has very poor leverage when lengthened and the best leverage when shortened.

so what is the best incline for this? a 60 degree incline will match the resistance profile and strength profile of the biceps making it so it's hardest around 30 degrees of elbow flexion making it better than a 45 degree bench incline where it will be harder within that 0-20 degree range.
