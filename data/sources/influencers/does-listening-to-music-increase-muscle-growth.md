# Does listening to music increase muscle growth?

Does listening to music increase muscle growth? Does the type of music you listen to increase performance?

many people listen to music while they workout but is this actually beneficial or is it a distraction?

it is actually shown that listening to faster tempo music increases performance during exercise/strength training.

sources:

besides tempo another reason why any music can help with performance is through motivation. this is because as your motivation is increased your perception of effort is lower which will increase motor unit recruitment.

More motor unit recruitment refers to the activation of a greater number of motor units within a muscle to increase the overall force of contraction.
