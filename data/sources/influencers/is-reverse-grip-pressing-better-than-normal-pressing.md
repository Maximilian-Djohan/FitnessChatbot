# Is reverse grip pressing better than normal pressing?

Is reverse grip pressing better than normal pressing?

when benching normally we will be performing the chest's main function of horizontal adduction. does anything change when using a reverse grip or is it just unsafe?

when using a reverse grip it wont inherently be dangerous especially if done on something like a smith. when using a reverse grip you are supinating which will naturally tuck your arms to your side. this makes it so you will have more shoulder flexion during your press which will give you more upper pec stimulus.

this doesnt mean its better but if you want more upper pec in your press reversing your grip can allow you to be tucked for that.
